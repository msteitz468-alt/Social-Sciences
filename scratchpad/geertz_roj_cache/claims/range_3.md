### ROJ3-001
- **Claim:** Geertz frames Islam as a religion of ethical prophecy: Muhammad’s break with tradition was sharp; message was rationalization and simplification (one God, limited polygamy, moderate asceticism, simplified ritual, universal message, holy war).
