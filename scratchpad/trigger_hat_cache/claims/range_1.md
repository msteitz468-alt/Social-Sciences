### C1-081: correspondence-and-coherence-truth
- **Register:** theoretical claim
- **Claim:** Archaeologists generally accept (mostly implicitly) two types of verification (Lowther; Kosso): correspondence truth (explanation matches facts—e.g., drought-collapse claim needs drought evidence) and coherence truth (logical consistency).
