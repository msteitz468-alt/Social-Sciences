### C6-003: functionalism vs processualism defined

- **Grounding quote:** "Functionalism is a synchronous approach that attempts to understand how systems operate routinely without accounting for major changes. Processual approaches seek to understand how and why such systems change irreversibly." (lines 13488–13491)
