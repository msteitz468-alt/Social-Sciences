Since the Celtic presence in Thrace lasted for only two generations, it is
unlikely to have left any substantial trace, nor is there any reason to suppose
that its material culture was closely comparable to that of the west central
European La Tene culture, The few La Tene objects recovered from Thrace
need be little more than gifts arriving by exchange from the west, and their dis-
tribution does not necessarily reflect that of the Celtic group.
