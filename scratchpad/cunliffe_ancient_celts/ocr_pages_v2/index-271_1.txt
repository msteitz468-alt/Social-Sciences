to build his power base, while at the same time

chose to pursue the war suggests a carefully devised

strategy designed to leave the principal markets of
central Gaul intact for the Roman entrepreneurs
while taking control of the entire northern coastal
region from the Loire to the Rhine. By doing this he
would open up new markets with the British Isles,
