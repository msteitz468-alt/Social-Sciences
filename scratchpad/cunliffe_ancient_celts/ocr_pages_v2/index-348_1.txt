28. Dressel 1 amphorae

Amphorae were commonly used to transport wine. After the middle of the second
century nc there developed an extensive trade in wine between the producers of
northern Italy and the distributors along the southern coast of France who dealt
with the Gauls further inland. Two characteristic types of amphorae mapped here,
Dressel types 1A and 1B, emphasize the major trade routes in operation

c.150 e and AD 10, The importance of rivers and the Atlantic are clearly apparent.
