### Claim 1: Sparse ice-age archaeology of South Asia
- **Register:** finding
- **Claim:** Mithen reports that post-LGM knowledge of Indian subcontinent settlement is extremely limited—widespread stone scatters but few radiocarbon dates, no known dwellings or burials, one Late Pleistocene hearth, and very few butchered-bone collections.
