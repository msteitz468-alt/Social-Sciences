## PIP-R4-001
**Claim:** As of W&O 2007, scholarly consensus held that Native Americans derive principally from Asian ancestors who entered the New World via Beringia/Alaska, a view anticipated by José de Acosta (c. 1590) and Thomas Jefferson (1781).
**Register:** finding (historiography of knowledge)
**Grounding:** Acosta proposed Asian origin by 1590; Jefferson described passage from Asia via narrow strait and physical resemblance of Indians to eastern Asians (excl. Eskimo/Aleut links to Greenland).
