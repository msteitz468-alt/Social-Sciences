### C6-051: Three basic freedoms stated; first two scaffold the third

- **Grounding quote:** "basic forms of social liberty which one might actually put into practice: (1) the freedom to move away or relocate from one’s surroundings; (2) the freedom to ignore or disobey commands issued by others; and (3) the freedom to shape entirely new social realities, or shift back and forth between different ones… The three basic freedoms have gradually receded… How did it happen?
