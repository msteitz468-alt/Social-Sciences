### Claim 1

### Claim 7

### Claim 16
- **Register:** `[META]`
- **Claim:** Their first paper under-accounted for the urge to totalize into “culture” or “civilization”; they treated the phase as operationally manageable and horizon/tradition as integrative without phases losing identity—but tradition was defined so broadly as to include “full cultural tradition,” a self-sufficient unit like an amplified phase, creating a contradiction.

### Claim 25
