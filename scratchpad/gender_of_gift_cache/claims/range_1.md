### C1-010: Gift as anthropology's Melanesian entry point

### C1-012: The question "gender of the gift" defined
- **Register:** theoretical
- **Claim:** Strathern contrasts conventional anthropology (gift exchange as categorically neutral behavior deploying gendered assets under men's control of women) with Melanesian construal (transaction itself is gendered; ability to transact stems from that gendering and the burden of carrying transactions). - **Grounding quote:** "To ask about the gender of the gift, then, is to ask about the situation of gift exchange in relation to the form that domination takes in these societies. It is also to ask about the 'gender' of analytical concepts, the worlds that particular assumptions sustain."
