### C3-042 Commodity alienation model: goods as property without social sources

### C3-079 Same-sex mediation replicates singular persons; cross-sex unmediated substitutes product
**Register:** T (Strathern)

### C3-080 Multiple/partible person as root reference; focus same-sex ↔ cross-sex not male ↔ female
