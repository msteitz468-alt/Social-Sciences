### C-2-001 | Darwin's Glen Roy marine-origin blunder
- **Register:** FINDING
- **Target pages:** meltzer-first-peoples-2009, meltzer-david
- **Claim:** Meltzer 2009 reports that Darwin (1838–39) argued the Parallel Roads of Glen Roy were marine wave-cut beaches formed as Scotland rose from the sea, published in the *Philosophical Transactions*, later admitting to Lyell he had been “smashed to atoms about Glen Roy”—one of his few major geological gaffes.
- **Grounding:** “his paper ‘one long gigantic blunder’… one of the few gaffes Darwin made”; marine-origin theory vs. glacial lake terraces.
- **Notes:** Historiography of Ice Age recognition, not peopling data.

### C-2-008 | Orbital cycles: eccentricity, obliquity, precession
- **Register:** FINDING
- **Target pages:** meltzer-first-peoples-2009, last-glacial-maximum
- **Claim:** Meltzer 2009 reports three orbital-forcing cycles: eccentricity ~100,000 years; obliquity (tilt) ~41,000-year cycles between 21.8° and 24.4° (most recent glacial ~22.95°; present 23.45°); precession ~23,000-year full circuit (perihelion now in NH winters; opposite ~10,000 years ago); combined they can swing insolation by upwards of ~20% (eccentricity alone ≤0.3%; 1° less NH tilt reduces insolation ~3–4%).
- **Grounding:** “Eccentricity varies over a 100,000-year period; the earth's tilt swings in 41,000-year cycles… full precession circuit takes approximately 23,000 years.”
- **Notes:** Mechanism for glacial pacing.

### C-2-009 | Glacier growth from reduced summer insolation at high latitudes

### C-2-010 | Oxygen isotopes as glacial ice-volume proxy
- **Register:** FINDING
- **Target pages:** meltzer-first-peoples-2009, radiocarbon-dating
- **Claim:** Meltzer 2009 reports that preferential evaporation of ¹⁶O vs heavier ¹⁸O, and locking of light oxygen in land ice, enrich ocean ¹⁸O/¹⁶O during glaciations; foraminifer shells (e.g., *Globigerina bulloides*) record seawater ratios; deep-sea mud and Greenland/Antarctic ice cores yield δ¹⁸O plus CH₄, CO₂, dust, ash, pollen, sea salt over hundreds of thousands to millions of years.
- **Grounding:** “the ratio of ¹⁸O to ¹⁶O in the ocean reveals how much ¹⁶O is locked up on land, and so provides a proxy for times of glaciation.”
- **Notes:** Method epistemic leverage for ice volume/climate.
