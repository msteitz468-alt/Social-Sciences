## How this list is built

- **Deepening, not re-foundation.** Classical Durkheim/Weber/Marx, Boas/Malinowski
  ethnography, Childe/Binford method, Possehl/Kenoyer Indus, etc. are already
  ingested. Items below close *next-tier* gaps from `wiki/overview.md` and thin
  areas on discipline pages.
- **Tiered within each discipline.** Tier 1 first (unlocks densest link graph);
  Tier 2–3 deepen regional, methodological, and contemporary fronts.
- **Both wiki layers.** Theory/method monographs feed `theories/`, `thinkers/`,
  `concepts/`, `methods/`; ethnographies, surveys, and site/regional syntheses feed
  `societies/`, `cultures/`, `sites/`, `phenomena/` and may earn `studies/`.
- **`[FLAG]`** — contested, partisan, or fast-superseding works. Heavy
  `reliability_notes`; interpretive theses on `debates/`; aDNA and similar get
  "(as of Author Year)" stamps.
- **`[X-disc]`** — cross-disciplinary; ingest once, link from both fields.

## Selection bias — stated honestly

Canon-weighted and anglophone-heavy, with a deliberate tilt toward **missing mid-
century and contemporary programs**, **Global South producers of theory**, and
**regional archaeological monographs** that textbooks only name. Still under-
represents non-English scholarship and post-2020 literature. Rebalance on request.
