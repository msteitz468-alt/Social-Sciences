# Cafer Höyük

PPNB village site in the Malatya / upper Euphrates zone of eastern Anatolia. Featured in [[cauvin-jacques|Cauvin]] (*[[cauvin-birth-of-the-gods-2000|2000]]*) as part of the **neolithisation of Anatolia** and illustrated for polished stone vase craft (plate references). French mission excavations; objects in Malatya Museum.

## Discovery and Excavation History

French archaeological mission; Cauvin cites results as part of the northern PPNB expansion story. Excavation quality standard for late-20th-c. research digs; details beyond Cauvin’s synthesis need specialist reports.

## Occupation Sequence

PPNB occupation with fine stone vessels and related material culture marking Anatolian participation in the aceramic Neolithic package (Cauvin’s framing).

## Significance

Extends the map of PPNB cultural traits beyond the Levantine corridor into Anatolia; craft evidence for specialised production.

## Key Debates

Timing relative to Early vs Middle PPNB expansion waves; local vs Levantine demic input.
