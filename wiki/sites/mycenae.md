# Mycenae

## Discovery and Excavation History

Type site of [[mycenaean-civilization]]; Schliemann's 1876 shaft-grave excavations launched modern Mycenaean archaeology (with all the methodological limits of that era). Later systematic Greek and international work refined palace and fortification sequences.

## Occupation Sequence

Rise to palatial power in LH; fortifications and palace; destruction in the late palatial period (LH IIIB horizon in standard ceramic chronology) with post-palatial continuation at reduced scale in the region.

## Significance

Namesake and elite center for Mycenaean culture; participant in LBA exchange networks; case in [[late-bronze-age-collapse]] narratives ([[cline-1177-bc-study]]).

## Key Debates

Absolute dating of destruction · agents of destruction · relation to Homeric tradition (analogy/literature, not automatic history).
