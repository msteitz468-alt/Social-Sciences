# Pylos (Palace of Nestor)

## Discovery and Excavation History

Mycenaean palace at Ano Englianos excavated by Carl Blegen; Linear B archive is a primary source for palatial administration. Modern restudy continues.

## Occupation Sequence

Palatial center destroyed c. 1180 BCE (conventional date in literature Cline uses). Jack Davis (cited by Cline) notes devastation so complete that palace and community did not recover; regional depopulation lasting centuries in that reconstruction.

## Significance

Clearest textual view of a Mycenaean palace economy; exemplary case of non-recovery after [[late-bronze-age-collapse]].

## Key Debates

Cause of fire/destruction · absolute chronology · how representative of other Mycenaean polities.
