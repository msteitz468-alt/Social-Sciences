# Theory Hub

Graduate-level analyses of major theoretical programs and the debates that
formed them. Depth here only — ordinary summaries live in `wiki/theories/`.

**Division of labor:** the `theories/` page owns the summary (core claims,
origins, proponents, critiques, status, dense links); the hub page owns the
depth (full intellectual context, conceptual architecture claim by claim,
empirical program hits and misses, verbatim key passages, debate history).

Each hub page links its summary via `theory_page`; the summary carries a
reciprocal link back. Link hub pages by full path:
`[[hubs/theory/[program-slug]|...]]`. Bare double-bracket `slug` (no path) always means the summary.

---

## Selection criteria

Programs that organized a generation's research (structural-functionalism,
structuralism, processual archaeology, practice theory, rational choice,
world-systems) or debates that restructured a field (processual vs.
post-processual, Writing Culture, the sociobiology wars). Exclude one-author
frameworks with no research community — those live on the thinker's pages.

---

## Analysis standard

Single source of truth (create on first hub ingest, then lock):

`templates/theory-analysis-template.md`

- **3,500–5,500+ words**
- Key-texts table; claims-vs-evidence matrix
- Verbatim key passages with analysis
- Honest empirical program (hits, misses, anomalies)
- **"What Survived"** section mandatory
- Nine canonical body sections (see `CLAUDE.md` Theory Hub)
