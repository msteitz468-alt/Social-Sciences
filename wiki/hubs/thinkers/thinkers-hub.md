# Thinkers Hub

Graduate-level intellectual biographies of the discipline-makers. Depth here
only — ordinary network-oriented summaries live in `wiki/thinkers/`.

**Division of labor:** the `thinkers/` page owns the summary (overview,
contributions, genealogy, critiques, historiography); the hub page owns the
depth (formation and training, phased career, verbatim primary-text analysis,
fieldwork/empirical base examined critically, reception, politics and ethics).

Each hub page links its summary via `thinker_page`; the summary carries a
reciprocal link back. Link hub pages by full path:
`[[hubs/thinkers/[person-slug]|...]]`. Bare double-bracket `slug` (no path) always means the summary.

Slug of the hub page must match `thinkers/[person-slug].md` exactly.

---

## Selection criteria

Reserved for discipline-makers (founders, program-builders, method-inventors)
**or** figures with rich primary material and consequential controversy (e.g.
Mead, Chagnon). Exclude figures adequately served by the summary page.

---

## Analysis standard

Single source of truth (create on first hub ingest, then lock):

`templates/thinker-analysis-template.md`

- **4,000–5,000+ words** (flex by significance)
- Works timeline table; concepts-introduced matrix
- Verbatim primary-text quotation with analysis
- Empirical base examined critically (neither hagiography nor demolition)
- Strong counterfactual genealogy treatment
- Nine canonical body sections (see `CLAUDE.md` Thinkers Hub)
