# Differential Space

Lefebvre’s name for the space that would (or embryonically does) overcome [[abstract-space|abstract space]] through accentuated difference and appropriation (*[[lefebvre-production-of-space-1991|The Production of Space]]*).

## Definition as Coined

*(Attributed — Lefebvre.)*

> “Thus, despite — or rather because of — its negativity, abstract space carries within itself the seeds of a new kind of space. I shall call that new space 'differential space', because, inasmuch as abstract space tends towards homogeneity, towards the elimination of existing differences or peculiarities, a new space cannot be born (produced) unless it accentuates differences.”

Differential space restores unity to functions, elements, and moments abstract space breaks up; ends localizations that shatter the individual/social body; distinguishes what abstract space identifies (e.g. social reproduction vs genitality). Later chapters: induced vs produced differences; margins, body, leisure, and counter-projects can explode abstract space into a space that is *other*; transitional orientation from production of *things in space* to production of *space* (use over exchange; appropriation over domination).

Political criterion: “A revolution that does not produce a new space has not realized its full potential.” Only class struggle generates differences not intrinsic to economic growth as system.

## Semantic History

Closes the book’s dialectic (absolute → abstract → openings of the differential). Reception sometimes merges it with “right to the city” and identity/difference theory — not always Lefebvre’s own framing.

## Operationalizations

Mostly programmatic (festivals, reappropriated places, counter-spaces, non-visual restoration of the body). Weak as a measurement construct.

## Applications

Critical-utopian horizon for urban movements; pairs with lived moment of the [[spatial-triad]].

## Critiques and Limitations

Vagueness/romanticism charges; under-specified transition; risk of labeling any “lively” place differential without production analysis.
