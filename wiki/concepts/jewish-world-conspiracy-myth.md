# Jewish World-Conspiracy Myth

The **myth of the Jewish world-conspiracy** is Cohn's name for the conviction that Jews — *all* Jews, everywhere — form a single conspiratorial body set on ruining and then dominating the rest of mankind. In *[[cohn-warrant-for-genocide-1967|Warrant for Genocide]]* he treats this myth as the core of the deadliest, genocidal form of [[antisemitism]], distinct from ordinary social prejudice or real conflicts of interest.

## Definition as Coined

**Attributed (Cohn):** a quasi-demonological superstition that reworks older Christian images of the Jew as agent of Satan/Antichrist into a modern political fantasy of occult global control (finance, press, revolution, freemasonry). The *[[protocols-of-the-elders-of-zion|Protocols]]* are its supreme modern **vehicle**, not its origin. Cohn's title claim: masters of a great European nation used the myth as a **warrant for genocide**.

## Semantic History

| Layer | Content (as Cohn reconstructs) |
|---|---|
| Patristic / medieval | Jews as demonic, Antichrist's people; blood libel; secret anti-Christian government tropes |
| Post-1789 | Barruel-style anti-Enlightenment conspiracy literature fused with anti-Jewish themes (Simonini letter lineage) |
| Mid–late 19th c. | Gougenot des Mousseaux; Alliance Israélite Universelle as supposed HQ; freemason–Jewish conflation; Goedsche Rabbi's Speech; Brafmann "Kahal" |
| Early 20th c. | Protocols paste-up; Russian pogromist and Okhrana milieus |
| Interwar | Global print culture; Ford; *völkisch* racial rewrite |
| Nazi | State propaganda + extermination warrant (Cohn's climax) |
| Post-1945 | Adapted forms (Cohn: Stalinist "imperialist plot" variants; fringe Western denialist ecology) |

Cohn stresses **continuity of structure** (occult cabal ruling events) across theological and racial idioms.

## Operationalizations

Content analysis of propaganda; edition and citation networks of forgeries; trial records (Berne); correlation with pogrom and policy episodes — always as **associated_with** / enabling ideology. Promoting the myth to sole `caused_by` of the Holocaust without mechanism is almost never defensible; Cohn himself pairs myth with crisis (war, revolution, slump) and later with racial ideology and state power.

## Applications

- Explanatory strand in Holocaust ideology research ([[cohn-warrant-for-genocide-study]], [[holocaust-and-modernity-debate]]).
- Comparative conspiracy-myth and [[millenarianism|millenarian]] studies (Antichrist/end-time packaging).
- Boundary work: distinguishes genocidal conspiracy antisemitism from other hostilities without collapsing all criticism of persons or states into the myth.

## Critiques and Limitations

Must not be collapsed into (a) all antisemitism, (b) all criticism of specific Jewish individuals or of Israeli policy, or (c) wiki-voice claims about Jewish collective behavior. It is an **analyzed ideology**, not a description of Jews. Causal sufficiency for the Holocaust is debated relative to bureaucracy, war-making, and other racisms — record Cohn, Bauman, and Goldhagen as **positions**, not as a single wiki verdict.

### American carriers (Dinnerstein 1994, attributed)

[[dinnerstein-antisemitism-in-america-study|Dinnerstein]] documents the myth's American lineage: Rothschild-conspiracy items in the US press from 1829 (*Niles' Register*: Rothschild as "the true king of Judah"); Populist-era "Rothschild"/"Shylock" money-conspiracy rhetoric; the 1909 "white slave trade" libel (George Kibbe Turner, who admitted under oath he had "no personal knowledge" of his claims); the post-1917 Jewish-Bolshevik equation; the Protocols' US arrival (1920) and Ford's *Dearborn Independent* serialization; Coughlin's Nazi-supplied documents and *Social Justice* Protocols reprints (1938); postwar circulation via Gerald L. K. Smith (Protocols mailed to every member of Congress, 1947) and hate-sheet networks; and late variants including the 1988 Cokely AIDS-conspiracy claims. See [[populism-antisemitism-debate]] for the historiographical dispute over the agrarian strand.

### Post-1945 career (Wistrich 2010, attributed)

[[wistrich-robert|Wistrich]] (*[[wistrich-lethal-obsession-2010|A Lethal Obsession]]*) documents the myth's later carriers: Soviet anti-Zionist literature 1967–1980s (claims of Zionist control of the US economy/media; Emelianov's "Zionist-Masonic" government theory); post-Soviet Russian nationalism (Shafarevich's *Russophobia*, "Judeo-Khazar" narratives); Western far-right "ZOG" (Zionist Occupied Government) discourse; "Jewish cabal/Israel lobby" tropes among Western politicians and media (Dalyell 2003, Tonge 2006, the Iraq-War "neocon cabal" theory); 9/11-as-Mossad conspiracy claims across Arab, Iranian, and Pakistani media; and the Hamas Charter's attribution of both world wars and the French and Bolshevik revolutions to Jewish direction. [[holocaust-denial]] is the myth's reflexive variant: the "hoax" itself becomes proof of Jewish world-control. Wistrich's reading of this corpus as one continuous mutated myth is his [THESIS]; each documented instance is textual fact.
