# Identity Fusion

## Definition as Coined

In White's (2021) CSR ritual chapters: **identity fusion** is a visceral sense of oneness with a group that motivates strong pro-group action; shared personally transformative (memorable, meaningful, self-defining) experiences — often imagistic rituals — are proposed to produce fusion.

## Semantic History

From social psychology into Whitehouse's modes program and extreme-ritual research.

## Operationalizations

Survey scales; online experiments; comparisons with military, extreme sports, twins (White method table).

## Applications

Functions of high-arousal ritual; suicide sacrifice and warfare bonding hypotheses (attributed research programs).

## Critiques and Limitations

Causal path from ritual to fusion to violence is contested; not all high-arousal ritual is religious; measurement of "fusion" varies.
