# Derivations (Pareto)

## Definition as Coined

[[pareto-vilfredo|Pareto]] named the variable element *b* that accounts for constant element *a* (**[[residues]]**) **derivations**: the mind’s work “accounting for *a*” (§§850–852, 868). Derivations comprise logical reasonings, unsound reasonings, and manifestations of sentiments used for derivation — “manifestations of the human being’s hunger for thinking” (§1401). They account for the **production and acceptance** of theories from the subjective standpoint (§1397).

Persuasive force, Pareto argued, comes mainly from sentiments (residues), not from logico-experimental grounds (§1397). Where there is no explaining, there is no derivation; animals act by instinct without them (§1400).

## Operationalizations

**Four classes** (§1419; genetic sketch §1400):

| Class | Name | Genera (summary) |
|---|---|---|
| I | Assertion | α facts / β sentiments / γ mix — simplest “because one ought” |
| II | Authority | α individuals / β tradition-custom / γ divine or personified |
| III | Accords with sentiments or principles | α sentiments / β individual interest / γ collective interest / δ juridical entities / ε metaphysical entities / ζ supernatural |
| IV | Verbal proofs | Indefinite/equivocal terms; incidental sentiments/epithets; multiple meanings; metaphor, allegory, analogy as “proof” (Ch X) |

Simplest form: pure precept / tautology (“We do thus and so because we do thus and so”) (§1400).

## Applications

- **History as “patter”:** Pareto argued institutional histories have often been histories of derivations (theologies for religions, ethical theories for morals, political theories for politics) rather than of residues (§1402).
- **Multiple interchangeable T for one residue B:** Replacing one derivation with another scarcely modifies social forms; contradictory derivations may coexist and reinforce (§2086).
- **Scientific stance:** Derivations are objects of study, never tools of persuasion for the logico-experimental investigator (§1403).
- **Elite/politics:** Speculators and parties change colours while joining “behind the scenes” — derivations track power ([[demagogic-plutocracy]], [[speculators-and-rentiers]]).

## Critiques and Limitations

- Everything can be reclassified as a derivation — risk of immune theory.
- Distinction residue/derivation is analytic; concrete speech blends them.
- “Verbal proofs” class is vast; borders on ordinary language philosophy *avant la lettre*.
