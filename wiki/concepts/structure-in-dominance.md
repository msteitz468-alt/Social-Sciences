# Structure in Dominance

## Definition as Coined

*(Attributed — [[althusser-louis|Althusser]], *[[althusser-for-marx-1965|For Marx]]*, esp. “Contradiction and Overdetermination” and “On the Materialist Dialectic”; Glossary.)*

**Structure in dominance** names the form of unity of a Marxist complex whole: not a Hegelian expressive totality (all levels phenomena of one essence) and not a flat pluralism of factors, but a **structured whole** of relatively autonomous instances (economic, political, ideological — and theoretical practice as a distinct practice) in which **one instance is dominant**, while the **economy determines in the last instance** *which* instance occupies the dominant place.

Related load-bearing formula (Althusser on Marx’s “two ends of the chain”):

> on the one hand, determination in the last instance by the (economic) mode of production; on the other, the relative autonomy of the superstructures and their specific effectivity.

And the famous gloss: the “lonely hour of the last instance never comes” — last-instance determination is not a calendar moment of pure economic causality.

**Glossary (Brewster / L.A.):** Base and superstructure must not be conceived as essence and phenomenon; State and ideology are not mere expressions of the economy; they are autonomous within a structured whole where one aspect is dominant, this dominance being determined in the last instance by the economy.

| Layer | Content |

| **Althusser 1962–65** | Complex whole; uneven development; principal/secondary contradictions (via Mao *On Contradiction*) |
| *Reading Capital* (1965; not yet primary-ingested) | Full formalization of instances and “determination last instance” apparatus |
| Anthropological structural Marxism | Kinship or religion may be **dominant** while economy remains last-instance determinant — “function as” relations of production ([[structural-marxism]], Godelier/Terray) |

## Operationalizations

Interpretive schema for reading a [[social-formation]]:

1. Identify practices/instances and their relative autonomy.
2. Identify which instance is **dominant** in the conjuncture or mode.
3. Trace how economic determination last-instance assigns that dominance.
4. Read contradictions as [[overdetermination|overdetermined]] by that structure (displacement vs condensation).

## Applications

- Charter distinction of [[structural-marxism]] from economism and from Hegelian Marxism / expressive totality ([[western-marxism]] in the Lukácsian sense is a related but different recovery of totality).
- Analysis of “survivals” and unevenness without reducing them to pure residues of an essence.
- State theory: superstructure has **specific effectivity** — link to [[relative-autonomy-of-the-state]] (concept’s classical case remains Marx’s *Brumaire*; Althusser generalizes the superstructure side).

## Critiques and Limitations

- **Economism returns by the back door?** Critics argue last-instance economic determination still privileges the economic as the real of the social (Sahlins’s [[practical-reason]] critique of structural Marxism).
- **Empty formalism:** without concrete analysis of a conjuncture, “structure in dominance” can become a redescription rather than an explanation (Althusser’s own demand that overdetermination be grounded in the concepts of the Marxist theory of history).
- **Humanist / historicist critique:** decentred structure evacuates collective subject and praxis.
- Full apparatus of *Reading Capital* (Balibar on modes of production, etc.) is **not** yet primary-ingested; this page is grounded in *For Marx*.
