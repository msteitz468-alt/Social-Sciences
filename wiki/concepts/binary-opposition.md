# Binary Opposition

## Definition as Coined

A **binary opposition** is a pair of contrasting terms (A / not-A) treated as the elementary unit of a meaning system, with meaning located in the *contrast* rather than in either term. The concept comes from the structural phonology of Roman Jakobson and the Prague School (distinctive features defined by oppositions such as voiced/voiceless); [[levi-strauss-claude|Lévi-Strauss]] made it the basic operation of mind across cultural domains, crediting Jakobson for the "theoretical inspiration" behind the *[[levi-strauss-elementary-structures-1949|Elementary Structures of Kinship]]* (1949).

## Semantic History

In the *Elementary Structures* Lévi-Strauss works through a chain of oppositions — nature/culture, giving/receiving, endogamy/exogamy, restricted/generalized exchange, prescriptive/preferential — treating their apparent opposition as dissolving under structural analysis. In later work (*The Savage Mind*, *Mythologiques*) the raw/cooked and comparable oppositions organize whole analyses of totemism and myth. The opposition is grounded in a [[psychic-unity-of-mankind|psychic unity]]: the same logical operation is held to be available to all human minds.

## Operationalizations

Identified by paired contrastive terms that a system uses to generate further distinctions; in kinship, the moiety division ([[dual-organisation]]) is the sociological realization; in myth, oppositions and their mediations are the units of analysis.

## Applications

The engine of [[structuralism]]: kinship (cross/parallel, giver/taker), classification and [[totemism]], myth, food, and art (Lévi-Strauss reads [[caduveo|Caduveo]] graphic style through nested oppositions — representation/abstraction, symmetry/asymmetry). See [[nature-culture-distinction]].

## Critiques and Limitations

Critics (including post-structuralists and practice theorists) argue binary analysis imposes a Western logical schema, privileges the analyst's oppositions over actors' categories, and reduces rich variation to a tidy algebra; Derrida's deconstruction targeted precisely the hierarchical binaries (speech/writing) structuralism relied on. Record it as the method's characteristic device, powerful and contested, not a demonstrated universal of cognition.
