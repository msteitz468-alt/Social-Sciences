# Economism

## Definition as Coined

In *[[lenin-what-is-to-be-done-1902|What Is to Be Done?]]* (1902), [[lenin-vladimir|Lenin]] treats **Economism** as the Russian form of a broader “new tendency” that bows to the **spontaneity** of the labor movement and degrades Social-Democratic politics to [[trade-union-consciousness|trade-union politics]]. He argued the nickname is **too narrow**: *Rabochaya Mysl* and *Rabocheye Dyelo*, as he read them, did not simply deny politics, but recognized only politics that arise spontaneously from economic struggle and **refused** to work out an independent Social-Democratic policy matching general socialist tasks and Russian conditions.

Lenin summarized the *Credo* as formulating Economism’s political division of labor: workers carry on the economic (more correctly **trade-union**) struggle; the Marxian intelligentsia merge with liberals for the political “struggle.” He linked illegal Economism to the Bernsteinian turn among most “legal Marxists,” which he said vulgarized Marxism, restricted the class struggle to narrow trade unionism and gradual reforms, and sought to convert the nascent labor movement into a “tail of the liberals.”

## Semantic History

| Layer | Sense (as this source frames it) |
|---|---|
| *Credo* / late-1890s Russian controversy | Explicit program: economic/trade-union struggle for workers; political work for intelligentsia with liberals |
| *Rabochaya Mysl* (as Lenin cites No. 1) | Workers “taking fate into their own hands”; “Politics always obediently follow economics”; retreat to pure trade-union struggle |
| *Rabocheye Dyelo* / Martynov (as Lenin frames) | Defend “freedom of criticism,” mass-movement determination of tasks, “giving the economic struggle itself a political character,” tactics-as-process |
| **Lenin 1902** | Nickname retained but redefined as **slavish cringing before spontaneity** / *khvostism* (tailism)—broader than pure economic-only politics |
| International frame (Lenin’s linkage) | Russian variant of Bernsteinism / opportunism; “concealed Bernsteinists” when reform demands are advanced as the *Iskra* disagreement |

Lenin insisted modern international opportunism shared social-political content under varied national forms; demanding “freedom of criticism” without naming Russian Bernsteinism’s fruits was, he argued, empty talk.

## Operationalizations

Lenin did not offer a survey scale. His operational markers (attributed) include:

- Elevating factory “exposures” and economic struggle against employers/government as the main or “most widely applicable” path into politics.
- Treating “giving the economic struggle a political character” as the struggle for **economic reforms** (trade-union politics via legislation/administration—what the Webbs, he noted, already described).
- Fear of publicity and of theoretical/organizational controversy; “our business is the labor movement… here, in our localities.”
- Organizational preference for wide, “easily understood” workers’ funds/rules over an [[professional-revolutionary|organization of revolutionaries]].
- Ambiguous thesis that the mass labor movement will “determine the tasks,” interpreted as subservience to spontaneity rather than new, more complex theoretical-political-organizational duties.

## Applications

In the *Preface to Twelve Years* ([[lenin-collected-works-vol-13-1962]]), Lenin re-narrated Economism as a stage in a continuous Right tendency (legal Marxism → Economism → Menshevism) — attributed, same volume’s retrospect.


- **Polemic targets in 1902:** *Credo* authors, *Rabochaya Mysl*, *Rabocheye Dyelo* (Krichevsky, Martynov), Self-Emancipation of the Workers Group manifesto (March 1899), League/Bund formulations on economic struggle as best political method, and the “economic” letter in *Iskra* No. 12.
- **Diagnostic of crisis:** Lenin held leaders lagged the spontaneous rise of the masses; Economist phrases about not “belittling the spontaneous element” **defended** primitive (*kustar*) methods and restricted political/organizational tasks.
- **Twin of “modern terrorism” (as Lenin argued):** both Economism and *Svoboda*-style “excitative” terror spring from subservience to spontaneity and enact the *Credo* split—workers’ economic struggle vs. intellectuals’ separate political efforts.
- **Conclusion of the pamphlet:** Lenin periodized Russian Social-Democracy’s third period (~1897/98 onward) as dispersion and degradation of SD to trade unionism; answer to “What is to be done?”: **“Liquidate the Third Period.”**

## Critiques and Limitations

- **Asymmetric source base:** this page’s definition is drawn from Lenin’s *Iskra* polemical reconstruction of Economists. Their self-descriptions (e.g. Krichevsky: all socialist forms, including Bernsteinists, stand on proletarian class interests; Martynov: respond to immediate interests, organic contact with the proletarian struggle) must be held as **counter-positions**, not absorbed into Lenin’s labels—see [[spontaneity-vs-consciousness-debate]].
- **Name vs. content:** Lenin himself said “Economism” inadequately conveys bowing to spontaneous politics rather than pure denial of politics; later readers who equate Economism only with “economic struggle only” understate his 1902 usage.
- **Anthology caution:** editorial headnotes may paraphrase Economism as mere scorn of “spontaneous” revolution; Lenin’s text is a sustained dispute over **theory, agitation, and organization**, not a simple anti-mass slogan.
- **Scope of this ingest:** grounded in [[lenin-what-is-to-be-done-1902]]; later Bolshevik/party-historiographical expansions of the label are not yet primary-filed here.
