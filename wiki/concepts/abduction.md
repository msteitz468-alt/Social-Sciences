# Abduction

## Definition as Coined

In C. S. Peirce’s logic of science, **abduction** is the mode of inference that **introduces a new idea** into inquiry. Tavory and Timmermans (*[[tavory-timmermans-abductive-analysis-2014|Abductive Analysis]]*, 2014) develop his contrast with:

| Mode | Movement (as T&T reconstruct Peirce) |
|---|---|
| **Deduction** | Rule → case → result that supports or falsifies the rule |
| **Induction** | Observed cases → strengthening/problematizing a universal rule (“ampliative”) |
| **Abduction** | Surprising result → speculative rule/case that would make the result “a matter of course” |

Peirce (as they quote) describes suddenly assuming something unknown so that a “confused snarl” of facts arranges itself luminously — that is abduction. Proposition *A* is **guessed after the fact**, not assumed beforehand (deduction) or ideally observed (induction).

## Semantic History

1. **Peirce:** creative inferential step; tied to semiotics and fallibilist community inquiry; refuses a hard firewall between “context of discovery” and “context of justification” (the Reichenbach/Popper division T&T attack).
2. **Mid-20th-century philosophy of science:** discovery sidelined as psychology/history; science identified with justification (verification or falsification). T&T: unhelpful for theorizing and a poor model of actual scientific work.
3. **Qualitative methods:** some grounded-theory advocates redescribe GT as abductive; T&T welcome the word but argue **rebranding** does not undo inductivist research design, coding primacy, and sidelining of theory after a nod to “sensitivity” (Strauss mentioned abduction once in a footnote; Glaser, they say, never broached it).
4. **[[abductive-analysis]] (2014):** full program — multiple theories, method-driven defamiliarization, variation, consequences-in-action, community of inquiry.

## Operationalizations

Not a statistical procedure. In the 2014 program:

- Maximize chances of **empirical surprise** (defamiliarizing methods; not immersion alone).
- Hold **multiple theoretical perspectives** so misfit is visible; unanticipated observations are strategic only for a **theoretically sensitized** observer ([[theoretical-sensitivity]] adjacent).
- Form candidate casings (“what is this a case of?”).
- Discipline candidates via **variation**, further data, and [[consequences-in-action]] causal tracing; cull via community tests of fit/plausibility/relevance.

## Applications

Theory construction in qualitative sociology and adjacent fields; explicit alternative to pure induction ([[grounded-theory]]) and single-theory extension ([[extended-case-method]]).

## Critiques and Limitations

- Without design constraints, collapses to “be creative.”
- Theoretical pluralism can mean canon gatekeeping.
- Contested continuity with later GT self-descriptions as already abductive (T&T: insufficient).
- Everyday actors also abduct; research differs by **cultivating** surprise, systematic sets, and communal standards.
