# Consciousness from Without

## Definition as Coined

In *[[lenin-what-is-to-be-done-1902|What Is to Be Done?]]* (1902), [[lenin-vladimir|Lenin]] asserted that Social-Democratic consciousness “could only be brought to [the workers] **from without**.” He grounded the claim in two moves:

1. **Historical-sociological:** the theory of socialism grew out of philosophic, historical, and economic theories elaborated by educated representatives of the propertied classes—the intellectuals. Marx and Engels, he noted, belonged by social status to the bourgeois intelligentsia. In Russia, Social-Democratic doctrine arose “quite independently” of the spontaneous labor movement, as outcome of ideas among the revolutionary socialist intelligentsia (Emancipation of Labor program by mid-1890s).
2. **Kautsky endorsement:** quoting Kautsky on the Austrian draft program, Lenin advanced that modern socialist consciousness arises only on the basis of profound scientific knowledge; vehicles of science are the bourgeois intelligentsia, not the proletariat; they communicated socialism to more developed proletarians who introduced it into the class struggle. Thus socialist consciousness is *von Aussen Hineingetragenes*—introduced from without—not something that arose *urwüchsig* (spontaneously) within the struggle. Social-Democracy’s task is to imbue the proletariat with consciousness of its position and tasks; there would be no need if consciousness emerged of itself from class struggle.

In Section III he **restated** the thesis in political-agitation terms: class political consciousness can be brought only from **outside the economic struggle** and the sphere of worker–employer relations—from relationships among **all** classes/strata and the state. Therefore Social-Democrats must “go among all classes of the people,” not merely “among the workers.”

## Semantic History

| Layer | Formulation |
|---|---|
| Kautsky (as Lenin quotes) | Socialism and class struggle arise **side by side**, not one out of the other; revisionists wrong to treat consciousness of socialism’s necessity as direct product of economic development/class struggle (England as counterexample) |
| Lenin II.A | No SD consciousness yet among workers in 1890s strikes; only [[trade-union-consciousness]] by exclusive own effort |
| Lenin after Kautsky block | No independent ideology developed by worker masses in their movement; **only** bourgeois or socialist ideology; no middle / non-class ideology under class antagonisms; to belittle socialist ideology is to strengthen bourgeois ideology |
| Lenin III.E | “From without” = outside economic struggle; ideal SD as **tribune of the people**, not trade-union secretary |
| Svoboda / “pushing from outside” (as Lenin answers) | “Pushing on from outside” has been **too little**, not too much; need committee of [[professional-revolutionary|professional revolutionaries]] |

Lenin held spontaneous movement follows the line of least resistance toward bourgeois ideology because that ideology is older, more fully developed, and has immeasurably more distribution opportunities—hence the younger a socialist movement, the more vigorously it must fight non-socialist ideology.

## Operationalizations

Practical corollaries Lenin drew (attributed):

- Organize **all-sided political exposure** of autocracy (not only factory oppression); living exposures “following hot after events,” not books alone.
- Develop workers’ capacity to observe every other class (landlord, priest, official, peasant, student, tramp) and apply materialist analysis—those who concentrate attention exclusively/mainly on the working class alone “are not Social-Democrats.”
- Propagandists, agitators, theoreticians, organizers among **all** strata; all-Russian Social-Democratic paper as national tribune of exposure.
- Train working-class revolutionaries to the same level of Party activity as intellectual revolutionaries; Party maintain talented worker agitators so they need not remain eleven hours in the factory.

## Applications

- **Combat of spontaneity:** Social-Democracy’s task, he argued, is to divert the labor movement from spontaneous trade-unionist striving under the bourgeoisie’s wing to revolutionary Social-Democracy’s wing.
- **Imagined advanced worker (Lenin’s rhetoric):** workers already run economic struggle; they demand political knowledge intellectuals must bring “in a hundred and a thousand times greater measure.”
- **Vanguard criterion:** only a party organizing real public exposures can become vanguard of revolutionary forces; label “vanguard” on rearguard practice is insufficient—see [[vanguard-party]].
- **Allies:** liberals and intelligentsia ready to join political exposures are needed for workers’ political training (also citing Axelrod 1897 as Lenin presents him)—against the Economist letter charging *Iskra* with abandoning the class point of view.

## Critiques and Limitations

- **Economist / spontaneist counter-position:** mass labor movement determines tasks; consciousness should not “divert” the movement from the path set by material elements and environment; *Iskra* “belittles the objective or spontaneous element”; first means of struggle are trade-union means and politics is superstructure on economic agitation (Self-Emancipation, *Rabochaya Mysl* as Lenin cites them).
- **Democratic-suspicion of “from without”:** later critics (beyond this source) treat the formula as authorizing substitution of party for class; Lenin’s text pairs it with raising workers to professional-revolutionary level, but the tension is constitutive of [[spontaneity-vs-consciousness-debate]].
- **Attribution hygiene:** the phrase is **Kautsky’s** in the long block Lenin quotes, then **Lenin’s** programmatic generalization; do not collapse Kautsky, Lenin, and later [[imputed-class-consciousness|Lukácsian imputation]] into one mechanism without argument (class-consciousness page already flags non-identity).
