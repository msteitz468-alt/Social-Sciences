# Social Physics

## Definition as Coined

**Social physics** is [[comte-auguste|Comte]]'s name for the science of social phenomena — the remaining gap in positive philosophy after astronomy, terrestrial physics, chemistry, and physiology (*Positive Philosophy*, Introduction ch. I; Book VI title). He also used **sociology** for the same science. Martineau's Book VI is titled "Social Physics" throughout.

Comte presented it as:

- the most particular, complex, and dependent science in the [[hierarchy-of-the-sciences]];
- still implicated with theologico-metaphysical philosophy in his day;
- necessary both to complete the encyclopedic scale and to address modern "intellectual anarchy" and the revolutionary condition of society (Book VI ch. I);
- dual in problem-structure: **Order** and **Progress**, addressed by [[social-statics]] and [[social-dynamics]].

Book VI ch. I argues the necessity and opportuneness of the new science; ch. II reviews prior philosophical attempts to constitute a social system (Montesquieu, Condorcet, etc.) as incomplete.

## Semantic History

- **Comte:** social physics ≈ sociology as positive science of order and progress.
- **Later:** "social physics" sometimes revived for quantitative/physics-inspired social science (different programs — do not silently identify with Comte).
- **Adolphe Quetelet** and others used related "social physics" language in statistical work — a parallel and sometimes competing usage; not yet ingested here.

## Operationalizations

Split into [[social-statics]] and [[social-dynamics]]; method chapters of Book VI (observation, comparison, historical method); historical application via the [[law-of-three-stages]] and the [[theological-military-system]] schema.

## Applications

Founding object of [[sociology]] as discipline page; Book VI of [[comte-positive-philosophy-martineau-1853]].

## Critiques and Limitations

Name encodes natural-science analogy (physics of society). Critics of scientism target this framing; defenders of scientific sociology may keep the ambition while dropping the label. Record positions when debate sources are ingested.
