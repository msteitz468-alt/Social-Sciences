# Withering Away of the State

## Definition as Coined

[[engels-friedrich|Engels]] concludes *[[engels-origin-of-the-family-1884|Origin of the Family]]* ch. IX by arguing that the [[the-state|state]] has not existed from all eternity; societies managed without it; at a stage of economic development involving class cleavage the state became necessary; production is approaching a stage where classes hinder production and will fall as they arose — “The state inevitably falls with them. The society which organizes production anew on the basis of free and equal association of the producers will put the whole state machinery where it will then belong–into the museum of antiquities, next to the spinning wheel and the bronze ax.” This **withering-away** thesis is a theoretical prediction and political horizon, not an empirical finding.

## Semantic History

Central to later Marxist state theory and to criticisms of twentieth-century socialist states that did not “wither.” Distinct from anarchist immediate abolition and from Weberian analyses of the modern state’s rationalization (no withering built in). Interacts with [[relative-autonomy-of-the-state]] debates: if the state can appear independent, does class abolition automatically dissolve it?

### Lenin’s dual formula (*State and Revolution*, 1917 — attributed)

[[lenin-vladimir|Lenin]] (*[[lenin-state-and-revolution-1917|State and Revolution]]*) makes a load-bearing distinction: the **bourgeois** state is **abolished** / **[[smash-the-state-machine|smashed]]** by revolution; only the **proletarian** state (a semi-state, the [[dictatorship-of-the-proletariat]]) **withers away**. He argues that popular and “opportunist” receptions collapse the whole of Engels into a harmonious “withering” that omits violent revolution and the smash of the ready-made machine. Economic basis (via Marx’s *Critique of the Gotha Programme*): lower phase of communism still carries “bourgeois right” and needs residual coercive/legal forms; complete withering opens only with the higher phase (“from each according to his ability, to each according to his needs”) as simple rules of social life become habit. Against anarchists (Engels *On Authority*, via Lenin): all socialists agree the state will disappear *as a result* of social revolution, but not “at one stroke” before the social relations that birthed it are destroyed. See [[marxist-theory-of-the-state]], [[smash-or-capture-the-state-debate]].

### *Left-Wing Communism* on the pace of withering (1920, attributed)

*[[lenin-left-wing-communism-1920]]* implicitly qualifies the withering horizon: Lenin describes the dictatorship of the proletariat as "a most determined and most ruthless war" against the "forces and traditions of the old society," including the constant, spontaneous regeneration of capitalism by small commodity production — a picture of protracted struggle in tension with any reading of withering as rapid or automatic once classes are formally abolished.

### 1921 extension: economic foundations for the withering away of bureaucracy (attributed)

In *[[lenin-tax-in-kind-1921]]* (Plan-of-Pamphlet notes), Lenin cross-referenced *State and Revolution* on "the economic foundations for the withering away of the state" and extended the concept: under NEP conditions there are also "economic foundations... for the withering away of bureaucracy" — but he stated plainly "the economic basis of socialism is not yet there," identifying the actual present basis as "commodity exchange with the peasantry." He traced the trajectory of Soviet [[bureaucracy]] in stages — smashed "from top to bottom" six months after October 1917; a Party Programme admission of "partial revival" by the Eighth Party Congress (March 1919); "even more distinct and sinister" evils by spring 1921 — arguing this reversion, unlike bourgeois-state bureaucracy (which he held exists to maintain a coercive apparatus against revolution), stemmed instead from the "atomised and scattered state of the small producer": poverty, illiteracy, and the absence of exchange between agriculture and industry, worsened by civil war and War Communism's "locking up" of exchange. This dates and qualifies the withering thesis: four years after the seizure of power, Lenin held the *economic* precondition for withering — developed large-scale production superseding small-scale — still had to be built, via state capitalism as an "intermediary link," before either the state or its bureaucratic distortions could wither.

## Operationalizations

Not operationalized as a measurable variable in *Origin*; it is a historical-materialist inference from the state’s alleged class origin. Lenin adds phase-structure from *Gotha* (lower/higher communism) without quantifying a withering schedule.

## Applications

*Origin* ch. IX; *[[lenin-state-and-revolution-1917|State and Revolution]]* (canonical short synthesis for the revolutionary Marxist tradition); reception in Leninist and anti-Leninist Marxism; contrast with [[weber-max|Weber]]’s monopoly-of-force definition on [[the-state]].

## Critiques and Limitations

Falsified as near-term prediction by actually existing socialist states for many critics; defenders treat it as conditional on genuine classlessness and world scale. Anarchist critique: transitional “workers’ state” never withers. Reformist critique: withering language excuses undemocratic dictatorship. Record as POSITION, not wiki-voice future.
