# North Atlantic Universals

## Definition as Coined

[[trouillot-michel-rolph|Michel-Rolph Trouillot]] names **North Atlantic universals** a family of words that project the North Atlantic ("West") experience onto a universal scale they themselves create — particulars that have gained a degree of universality. He prefers "North Atlantic" to "West" to localize/parochialize the experience (the West as project, not place — via Glissant).

> "North Atlantic universals are words that project the North Atlantic experience on a universal scale that they themselves have to create. … North Atlantic universals are particulars that have gained a degree of universality, chunks of presized human history that have become historical standards." (Trouillot 2004)

Trouillot further argues: progressive scholars often relativize only politically suspect packages while leaving classificatory universals (class, race, gender) under-historicized; adding non–North Atlantic *facts* is not the same as studying the world ("we have never been universal"); race has a longue-durée conceptual career from Caribbean colonial description to biologization, with Boas-era US anthropology inventing race-as-culture as anticoncept that later re-exports via consumer culture (all attributed).

## Semantic History

Trouillot's contribution to the longue-durée analysis of concepts in the modern world-system (2004 volume). Overlaps decolonial critiques of universalism and Santos's epistemologies of the South without being identical. Related but not identical to [[traveling-universals|Tsing's traveling / engaged universals]] (*Friction*, 2005): Trouillot historicizes North Atlantic particulars-as-standards; Tsing ethnographizes how universalizing projects travel through friction in Indonesian cases.

## Operationalizations

Heuristic split: substantive universals (content) vs classificatory universals. Methodological and political relevance claimed by Trouillot; not a quantitative index.

## Applications

Critique of social-science and everyday categories that naturalize North Atlantic history as world history.

## Critiques and Limitations

Scope of the category can become all-encompassing; criteria for "universalized particular" need case-by-case argument.
