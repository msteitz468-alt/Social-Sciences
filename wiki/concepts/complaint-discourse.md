# Complaint Discourse

## Definition as Coined

*(Attributed — Harriet Rosenberg, Ch.7 of [[lee-richard-b|Lee]], *[[lee-dobe-juhoansi-2013|The Dobe Ju/'hoansi]]* 4th ed.)* **Complaint discourse** is the Ju/'hoan practice of loud, humorous, melodic complaining about caregiving failures, aging burdens, and unmet reciprocal duties. Rosenberg treats it as a **rhetoric of reciprocity** — "complaining greases the wheels" of care — not as mere grumbling. It sits beside meat-insult and stinginess charges as a speech technology of egalitarian obligation ([[leveling-mechanisms]]).

## Semantic History

Missing from Lee's 1979 ecology monograph; added as a full chapter from the **3rd edition (2003)** of the Dobe case study, reflecting Rosenberg's aging/caregiving research (funded work on aging among the Ju). Brought life-cycle and medical-anthropology angles into the textbook that students meet before the denser *!Kung San*.

## Operationalizations

- Emic speech labels: *horehore* / *obaoba* ("yakity-yak" argument talk also used in conflict chapters); elder entitlements naturalized as *da*, *!gu*, *msi* (food/water/care claims).
- Case method: Kasupe trap-story and other melodrama-vs-record comparisons; care networks of frail elders (Rosenberg: multiple carers per frail person, care **not** feminized to women alone).
- Comparative frame: Ju elders embedded in demanding kin networks vs North American elderly isolation and under-complaint.

## Applications

- **Aging under foraging and post-foraging conditions:** past *n/a a tsi*-type abandonment stories vs mid-1980s settled care.
- **Leveling and entitlement:** complaint polices stinginess in care the way [[insulting-the-meat]] polices hunting arrogance.
- **Teaching ethnography:** models how discourse analysis densifies a classic ecological case.

## Critiques and Limitations

Chapter depth is textbook-scale; denser Rosenberg primary publications may refine mechanisms. Do not universalize "complaint as health" beyond the Ju cases offered. Date claims to Rosenberg/Lee fieldwork moments, not to a timeless Ju psyche.
