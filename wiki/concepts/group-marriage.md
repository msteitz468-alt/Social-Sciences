# Group Marriage

## Definition as Coined

In [[morgan-lewis-henry|Morgan]]’s sequence as used by [[engels-friedrich|Engels]], **group marriage** (Morgan’s punaluan family; Lubbock’s “communal marriage”) is a supposed historical stage in which whole groups of men and women had reciprocal sexual rights, intermediate between the hypothetical consanguine family and the [[pairing-family|pairing family]]. Engels treats surviving classificatory kinship terms and certain customs as **survivals** proving the stage.

**From *[[morgan-systems-consanguinity-1871|Systems]]* (1871) primary:** Morgan’s conjectural series places communal family and Hawaiian [[punalua|pinalua]] custom before tribal exogamy and pair marriage; he uses Malayan [[classificatory-system-of-relationship|classificatory]] terminology as “preserved evidence” of that state (attributed; General Results). Full “punaluan family” stage labeling is sharper in *Ancient Society* (not yet ingested).

## Semantic History

Nineteenth-century evolutionism required a ladder from “promiscuity” through group marriage to monogamy. Later ethnography reanalyzed many cited cases as secondary forms, ritual, or misread kinship terminology — not fossils of a universal stage. The term survives mainly in history-of-ideas and Marxist canons.

## Operationalizations

Morgan *Systems*: Hawaiian missionary reports + Malayan schedule mergers; Engels/Morgan later: “punalua” relations as group. Evidence class: second-hand ethnography + kinship-term inference — weak for reconstructing deep prehistory.

## Applications

*Origin* ch. II builds the family sequence on group marriage’s rise and restriction; exogamy of the [[gens]] is tied to the punaluan reform (attributed).

## Critiques and Limitations

Core target of the [[unilinear-kinship-evolution-debate]]. Kinship terms do not uniquely encode past group-marriage norms; many societies lack any such stage in their reconstructible past.

**Lowie (*[[lowie-primitive-society-1920|Primitive Society]]*, 1920):** sexual communism / group marriage as equal multi-male multi-female partnership is not established as a world stage; cases of wife hospitality, ceremonial license, or Dieri-type concubinage coexist with individual marriage and do not equal Morgan’s package; levirate and sororate are not reliable fossils of prior group marriage; strict promiscuity in recent millennia is rejected (reaffirmed in 1947 Preface). See [[lowie-primitive-society-study]].
