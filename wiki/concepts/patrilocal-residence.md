# Patrilocal Residence

## Definition as Coined

**Patrilocal residence**: the married couple lives with or near the **husband's** people. Pair concept with [[matrilocal-residence]] in [[lowie-robert-h|Lowie]]'s family analysis (*[[lowie-primitive-society-1920|Primitive Society]]*).

## Semantic History

Standard residence typology; not identical with patrilineal [[sib]] or patriarchal authority.

## Operationalizations

As for matrilocal — specify duration, household head, and any bride-service exceptions.

## Applications

*[[lowie-primitive-society-study|Primitive Society]]* family and women's-status chapters.

## Critiques and Limitations

Same as matrilocal: label vs household process.
