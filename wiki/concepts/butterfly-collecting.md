# Butterfly Collecting (Leach)

> Methodological pejorative coined by [[leach-edmund|E. R. Leach]] in the 1959 Malinowski Memorial Lecture, published as Chapter 1 of *[[leach-rethinking-anthropology-1961|Rethinking Anthropology]]* (1961). Foil for his preferred program of **generalization** over organizational ideas as mathematical patterns.

## Definition as Coined

*(Attributed — Leach 1959/61.)* **Comparison**, in the Radcliffe-Brownian sense of arranging social structures by structural type (as one classifies sea shells), is **butterfly collecting**: classification of things according to types and subtypes. Leach associates this taxonomic habit with followers of [[radcliffe-brown-a-r|Radcliffe-Brown]] and contrasts it with **generalization** — inductive “inspired guesswork” about pattern regularities that may discover something new rather than restating known form.

Core methodological slogan (attributed): “Instead of comparison let us have generalization; instead of butterfly collecting let us have inspired guesswork.” The alternative is to treat organizational ideas in any society as constituting a **mathematical pattern** (topology / connectedness / ratios among neighbouring relationships — e.g. filiation variables *p* and *q*), not a natural species or organism to be typed.

## Semantic History

| Layer | Use |
|---|---|
| 1959 Malinowski Memorial Lecture | Load-bearing foil; harsh black/white contrast with Lévi-Strauss-style structural generalization (Leach accepts full blame for the caricature) |
| 1961 volume | Title essay of the collection; program statement for the kinship essays that follow |
| Later disciplinary talk | Becomes a standard catchphrase for anti-typological, model-oriented British anthropology after Leach; often detached from the full topology argument |

Not a school name — a **critique-label**. Do not treat “butterfly collecting” as a self-description of Radcliffe-Brown’s program; it is Leach’s polemic.

## Operationalizations

Leach does not operationalize butterfly collecting as a countable method. He illustrates the **failure mode**:

- Selecting cases by a prior type (e.g. only matrilineal systems) and then “discovering” that matriliny explains the variance — **tautology** (his reading of Audrey Richards’s comparative matriliny essay).
- Fitting ethnography into a priori categories (maximal/major/minimal lineages; complementary filiation; residual siblings) — “cycles and epicycles” analogous to Ptolemaic astronomy (citing Firth on terminological proliferation).
- Universal definitions (Gough on marriage; Fortes on filiation/affinity/descent) whose butterfly-collecting value is “ephemeral.”

Positive counterpart: topological analogy (rubber-sheet geometry) — stretch a figure of relationships until surface shape is unrecognizable while pattern of connectedness remains; generalized structural patterns are not restricted to one manifest type.

## Applications

- Frames Ch.1 continuum argument: Trobriand / intermediate / Kachin–Lakher as extremes of a variable pattern (incorporation vs alliance; mystical influence vs controlled attack), not three species of society.
- Underwrites the algebraic Jinghpaw analysis (Ch.2) and the matrilateral cross-cousin “function in a mathematical sense” (Ch.3).
- Feeds later textbook accounts of Leach as internal critic of British [[structural-functionalism]] and as ally of [[structuralism|Lévi-Straussian]] comparison-as-generalization.

## Critiques and Limitations

- **Caricature risk.** Leach himself notes that Lévi-Strauss might repudiate the authorship of ideas presented in “harsh and exaggerated form.”
- **Not a ban on comparison.** He rejects *typological* comparison of structures as natural kinds, not all controlled comparison; his own essays are densely comparative.
- **Pseudo-mathematics.** He later flags non-numerical “pseudo-mathematics”; the topology metaphor is heuristic, not formal topology.
- **Reception.** Descent theorists and comparative Africanists treated the lecture as an attack on cumulative typology rather than a pure method proposal — record as position, not wiki-voice verdict.
