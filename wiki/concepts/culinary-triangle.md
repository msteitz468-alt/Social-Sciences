# Culinary Triangle

> Densified from *[[levi-strauss-miel-aux-cendres-1966|Du miel aux cendres]]* (Mythologiques II) and the series frame of *Le Cru et le Cuit* (Vol. I not yet primary-ingested). Author: [[levi-strauss-claude]]. Study: [[levi-strauss-miel-aux-cendres-study]].

## Definition as Coined

*(Attributed — Lévi-Strauss.)* The **culinary triangle** names the structural analysis of how cultures classify and transform food. Elementary poles include **raw**, **cooked**, and **rotted** (culture’s path of cuisine vs nature’s path of spoilage), elaborated by operators such as roast, boil, and smoke. Cuisine mediates [[nature-culture-distinction|nature and culture]]; myths think with these categories.

In *Du miel aux cendres*, Lévi-Strauss extends the model to **entours du repas** (surroundings of the meal):

- **Honey — infra-culinary:** elaborated by non-humans, absorbed raw (or spontaneously fermented), on the near side of cooking.
- **Tobacco — meta-/ultra-culinary:** incinerated so that smoke is inhaled; beyond cooking toward offering and the supernatural.

## Operationalizations

| Term | Role (attributed — L-S) |
|---|---|
| Raw / cooked / rotted | Core triangle of natural vs cultural transformation |
| Infra-culinary (honey) | Ready-made; seduction of nature; wet/vegetable “natural cooking” paradox |
| Meta-culinary (tobacco smoke) | Ultra-cooked; offering; mediation with supernaturals |
| Roast / boil / smoke | Differentiated techniques in the operations system (figured in Vol. II) |
| Acoustic analogues | Noise instruments as convertible with alimentary codes (*instruments des ténèbres*) |

## Applications

South American myth groups in *Mythologiques*; European honey/tobacco metaphors used only as framing. Links to the claim that mythical thought can rise from a **logic of qualities** to a **logic of forms** without evolutionary necessity ([[levi-strauss-miel-aux-cendres-study]] coda).

## Critiques and Limitations

- Cell assignment of myths is analyst-dependent.
- Vol. I primary text not yet ingested — full derivation of the triangle incomplete in this wiki.
- Standing *Mythologiques* critique: transformation method risks unfalsifiability (see [[structuralism]]).
