# Spontaneity vs Consciousness (Economism Debate)

Russian Social-Democratic controversy of the late 1890s–early 1900s over the relative weight of the **spontaneous** labor movement and **conscious** Social-Democratic leadership. Positions recorded in strongest form from *[[lenin-what-is-to-be-done-1902|What Is to Be Done?]]* (1902) and interlocutors Lenin quotes. **No adjudication** in wiki voice. Related: [[economism]], [[trade-union-consciousness]], [[consciousness-from-without]], [[professional-revolutionary]], [[vanguard-party]], [[class-consciousness]].

## Shared factual ground (framed per side)

Both sides treat as given (while disagreeing on evaluation):

- Mass industrial labor movement and strike wave in 1890s Russia; proliferation of SD circles, illegal papers, factory leaflets.
- Polemics among *Iskra*/*Zarya*, *Rabocheye Dyelo*, *Rabochaya Mysl*, the *Credo*, League Abroad, Bund formulations, local rules and letters.
- International backdrop Lenin invokes: legal Marxism’s alliance and rupture; Bernstein’s “critical” turn; Millerand’s ministerialism.
- Autocratic repression repeatedly destroyed circles; legalization experiments (e.g. Zubatov-type unions, as Lenin discusses) underway.
- Mid-1890s Social-Democrats (as Lenin claims for continuity) combined economic agitation with political tasks against autocracy—disputed whether later practice rightly narrowed or wrongly abandoned that breadth.

One side reads these facts as proof leaders must catch mass energy with more consciousness and organization; the other as proof “subjective plans” and ideology-first politics lag or distort the real movement.

## Positions

### 1. Lenin / *Iskra* — consciousness must lead; combat spontaneity

**Strongest form:** The “spontaneous element” is consciousness only in **embryonic** form. Workers by exclusive own effort develop only [[trade-union-consciousness]]; Social-Democratic / class-political consciousness is brought [[consciousness-from-without|from without]] (Kautsky’s *von Aussen* formula as Lenin endorses it). Spontaneous labor-movement development leads to subordination to **bourgeois** ideology (*Nur-Gewerkschaftlerei*); no third, independent mass ideology under class antagonisms. Social-Democracy must **combat** spontaneity, divert the movement under revolutionary SD’s wing, and raise it to the program—not drag at the tail (*khvostism*).

The greater the spontaneous mass uprising, Lenin argued, the **greater** the demand for consciousness in theoretical, political, and organizational work; leaders lagging masses is the principal crisis cause. Political education requires all-sided exposure of autocracy among **all** classes; ideal leader is tribune of the people, not trade-union secretary. Organizationally: stable [[professional-revolutionary|organization of professional revolutionaries]]—secret, selected, continuous—plus all-Russian political organ. “Without a revolutionary theory there can be no revolutionary movement.” Pamphlet answer: **liquidate the third period** of vacillation and trade-unionist degradation.

### 2. *Rabochaya Mysl* / pure labor-movement tendency

**Strongest form (No. 1 leading article, as Lenin quotes):** Movement’s virility comes from workers taking fate **into their own hands** and out of leaders’ hands. Watchwords: economic position; “workers for the workers”; strike funds “more valuable… than a hundred other organizations”; average/mass worker not “cream.” “Politics always obediently follow economics.” A kopek on the ruble, and struggle for themselves and children, outweigh abstract socialism for future generations.

**Coherent position:** trust spontaneous labor movement to set direction; suspect non-labor (even socialist) intelligentsia leadership; keep organization and agitation close to immediate economic struggle. (Lenin’s rebuttal—that this executes the *Credo* and echoes bourgeois pure-trade-unionism—is Position 1, not this side’s self-description.)

### 3. *Rabocheye Dyelo* / Krichevsky–Martynov — do not belittle the spontaneous element

**Strongest form:**

- **Krichevsky:** Mountain/Gironde talk is shallow analogy; modern socialism has **no conflict of class interests**—even pronounced Bernsteinists stand on proletarian class interests. Revolutionary SD only **accelerates objective development**; must not substitute subjective plans; *Iskra* belittles the objective/spontaneous element.
- **Martynov / RD:** Cannot confine oneself to exposing the system; must answer **immediate interests** of the proletariat; organic contact with the proletarian struggle vs *Iskra* as exposure literature of revolutionary opposition. Give the economic struggle itself a **political character**; economic struggle as widely applicable path into politics; concrete legislative/administrative demands with palpable results.
- **Tactics-process:** A tactics plan “contradicts the fundamental spirit of Marxism”; tactics grow with the Party. “That struggle is desirable which is possible, and the struggle which is possible is the one that is going on at the given moment.”
- **Unity:** “Freedom of criticism” as condition of durable unity abroad.

**Coherent position:** Marxism requires humility before objective mass development; over-planned “subjective” tactics and intolerance toward critical/reformist shades split the movement and disconnect leaders from workers’ real struggle.

### 4. *Credo* / Self-Emancipation stages

**\*Credo\* (as Lenin states its frank program):** Workers do economic/trade-union struggle; Marxian intelligentsia merge with liberals for political struggle.

**Self-Emancipation manifesto (March 1899, as Lenin summarizes):** Awakening workers clutch first means of struggle to hand; political agitation must be **superstructure** on economic agitation—arise on its basis and follow it.

**Coherent position:** respect stages; do not overload an awakening class with full SD political program before economic self-activity matures.

### 5. *Svoboda* / excitative terror (linked pole)

**Strongest form (as Lenin reports):** Terror’s role can be **excitative**—stimulating revolutionary energy—rather than primarily deterrent; when mass agitation intensifies, that function ends.

**Lenin’s linkage (Position 1 framing, not Svoboda’s self-ID):** Economists bow to spontaneity of pure labor movement; modern terrorists to spontaneity of intellectual indignation unlinked to labor; both enact the *Credo* split. Calls for terror and for “giving the economic struggle a political character” are twin evasions of all-sided political agitation.

### 6. “Economic” letter in *Iskra* No. 12

**Letter’s charge:** *Iskra* overestimates ideology, seeks liberal/intelligentsia allies without patience for workers to accumulate forces, departs from class point of view, obscures antagonisms, puts general discontent first.

**Lenin’s reply (Position 1):** Accumulating forces **is** political training via all-sided exposure; allies for exposures are needed; Economists reverse the order (trade-union politics first).

## Current status

`resolution_status: open`. Bolshevik victory narratives long treated the Lenin side as orthodoxy; Menshevik, Western Social-Democratic, anarchist, council-communist, and later democratic-socialist and scholarly literatures keep the 1902 theses contested (theory of consciousness; hierarchical party form; conjunctural argument under tsarism). This wiki does **not** declare a winner. Further primary ingests (Economist texts in their own right, 1903 split materials, later Lenin, critical historiography) should expand positions without overwriting this source-grounded record.
