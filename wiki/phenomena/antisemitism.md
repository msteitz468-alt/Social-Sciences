# Antisemitism

**Antisemitism** denotes hostility, prejudice, and structured discrimination directed at Jews as Jews — spanning religious [[anti-judaism]], modern racial antisemitism, and political conspiracy myths. This page is densified from [[cohn-norman|Norman Cohn]]'s *[[cohn-warrant-for-genocide-1967|Warrant for Genocide]]* (1967), cross-linked to [[genocide]] / [[holocaust-and-modernity-debate]] ([[bauman-zygmunt|Bauman]]), and further densified from [[nirenberg-david|David Nirenberg]]'s *[[nirenberg-anti-judaism-2013|Anti-Judaism]]* (2013). It is **not** a complete world history of antisemitism.

## Definition and Scope

Cohn (attributed) distinguishes ordinary prejudice and interest-conflict from a **deadly, genocidal** variant organized by the [[jewish-world-conspiracy-myth]]: the belief that Jews worldwide form a conspiratorial body bent on domination. The *[[protocols-of-the-elders-of-zion|Protocols of the Elders of Zion]]* are that myth's supreme modern vehicle. Bauman (attributed) emphasizes modern **racism** and bureaucratic machinery over subterranean myth alone. Nirenberg (attributed) adds a third axis: **[[anti-judaism]]** as a long Western habit of *thinking with* Jewish figures — not identical with modern racial antisemitism, and not a simple continuous hatred that “causes” the Holocaust, but a history without which modern genocidal antisemitism was (he argues) inconceivable. These are explanatory positions; none monopolizes wiki voice. Boundary dispute: [[anti-judaism-continuity-debate]].

## Measurement

Historical: laws, expulsions, pogrom chronologies, propaganda corpora, forgery edition histories (Cohn's method); intellectual-historical corpora of scripture, law, drama, and philosophy (Nirenberg's method). Modern: surveys, hate-crime statistics, discourse analysis. Boundary disputes (anti-Judaism vs. antisemitism; anti-Zionism debates) are live and partly outside any single source.

## Patterns and Findings

- **Demonological lineage (Cohn, attributed):** Patristic and medieval images of Jews as Satan/Antichrist agents feed modern conspiracy forgeries; the structure (occult cabal) persists when the idiom shifts from theology to race.
- **Crisis amplification (Cohn, attributed):** war, revolution, and economic slump allow subterranean myths to capture mass publics and state actors; without those crises the Protocols would have stayed obscure, but without the myth the crises would not (he argues) have produced Auschwitz.
- **Organizational carriers (Cohn, FACT as he reconstructs):** Okhrana and Black Hundreds milieus; White armies; interwar parties and presses (including Ford's *International Jew*); Nazi propaganda apparatus; wartime collaboration administrations.
- **Violence scale (Cohn reports, not independent census):** Russian civil-war massacres of Jews over 100,000 in his account; Holocaust five to six million European Jews killed (his foreword figures), with fanatical targeting he treats as myth-structured.
- **Modern organization (Bauman, attributed):** genocidal outcome also requires bureaucratic capacity and state "gardening" ambition — [[genocide]], [[gardening-state]].
- **Thought-tools / mask history (Nirenberg, attributed):** pre-modern and modern cultures use figures of Judaism (letter, law, money, formalism, intellectualism) to think non-Jewish problems; [[judaizing]] is a transferable accusation; the Holocaust was **inconceivable without** this deep history but **not caused by** it (see [[anti-judaism]], [[anti-judaism-continuity-debate]]).
- **Critical thought colludes (Nirenberg, attributed):** academic and philosophical critique can share anti-Judaism’s cognitive claims (e.g. “Jewish formalism,” “Jewish capital”) — the problem is not only popular prejudice.

## The American Case (Dinnerstein 1994)

Dinnerstein (attributed; full treatment at [[dinnerstein-antisemitism-in-america-study]] and [[american-jews]]) argues American antisemitism is the transplanted Christian heritage in weakened form — no pogroms, no respectable antisemitic parties, no federal anti-Jewish laws — following a documented arc: colonial-era legal disabilities; the Civil War as the worst period to that date (Grant's General Orders No. 11, 1862); a "full-fledged antisemitic society" 1865–1900 (social exclusion, agrarian conspiracy rhetoric — see [[populism-antisemitism-debate]]); racialized antisemitism in the Progressive era ([[scientific-racism]]); a 1919–1945 crest (university quotas, 1921/1924 immigration restriction, Ford's *International Jew*, Coughlin, wartime polls peaking at 58% affirming "Jews have too much power" in 1945); then steep postwar decline, with the South ([[blood-libel]] incidents, the Leo Frank lynching, 1957–67 synagogue bombings) and post-1960s [[black-jewish-relations]] as the contested variations. Refugee-policy entanglement: [[fdr-holocaust-rescue-debate]].

- **Continuity / mutation thesis (Wistrich, attributed):** [[wistrich-robert|Wistrich]] (*[[wistrich-lethal-obsession-2010|A Lethal Obsession]]*, 2010) argues antisemitism did not recede after 1945 but mutated: its post-2000 dominant form is organized around Israel as the "collective Jew" ([[new-antisemitism]]), with Soviet anti-Zionism and Islamist ideology as the main transmission channels. Documentary spine (attributed facts): the term "antisemitism" coined by Wilhelm Marr (1879); racialization by ~1900 (Marr, Dühring, Chamberlain); Soviet campaigns (anti-cosmopolitan purge 1948–53, Doctors' Plot, Slánský trial, post-1967 anti-Zionist propaganda apparatus); post-communist "Judeo-Bolshevism"/żydokomuna revivals; and a dense post-2000 incident/survey record (CST, CNCDH, ADL series — each a dated single measurement). His continuity claim itself is Position 1 of [[new-antisemitism-debate]], in direct tension with Nirenberg's anti-teleology.
- **Carrier variants (Wistrich, attributed):** left/socialist antisemitism (Marx's 1844 essay, Proudhon, Bakunin, the "socialism of fools"); Islamist antisemitism fusing Koranic anti-Jewish motifs with imported European conspiracy material (Qutb's "Our Struggle with the Jews," Hamas Charter, Khomeinist Iran) — whether this current is indigenous or imported is itself disputed (el-Baz vs. Lewis/Wistrich positions); and [[holocaust-denial]] as the post-1945 conspiracist form.
