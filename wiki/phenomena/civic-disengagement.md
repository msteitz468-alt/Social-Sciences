# Civic Disengagement (U.S. late 20th century)

The claimed multi-domain decline of American [[civic-engagement]] and [[civic-social-capital]] from mid-twentieth-century peaks into the 1990s, as synthesized by [[putnam-robert-d|Putnam]] in *[[putnam-bowling-alone-2000|Bowling Alone]]*.

## Definition and Scope

*(Attributed — Putnam 2000.)* Not a single behavior but a syndrome across politics, clubs, religion, workplace, informal ties, altruism, and trust. Title metaphor: bowling participation up, league bowling down.

## Measurement

Triangulated survey and membership indicators (Appendix I). Independent reanalyses and alternative series belong on [[bowling-alone-debate]].
