# Boxing gym

## Definition and Scope

A **boxing gym** is a recurrent social form: dedicated training space with coaches, bags/ring, and amateur and/or professional membership, organized around transmission of pugilistic craft. Seeded from Wacquant’s analysis of the [[woodlawn-boys-club|Woodlawn Boys Club]] (Chicago, 1988–92); commercial and cross-national variants remain thin until further sources are ingested.

## Cross-Cultural Variation

*Body & Soul* is a single-site U.S. black urban traditionalist gym. Wacquant trained later in Boston, New York, Oakland and visited clubs in the U.S. and Europe, but the wiki’s primary case is Woodlawn. Do not generalize gym-as-moral-island without multi-site evidence.
