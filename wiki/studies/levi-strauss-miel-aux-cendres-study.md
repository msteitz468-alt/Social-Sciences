# Du miel aux cendres — Study

> Source: [[levi-strauss-miel-aux-cendres-1966]]. Author: [[levi-strauss-claude]]. Theory: [[structuralism]]. Concepts: [[myth]], [[culinary-triangle]], [[binary-opposition]], [[nature-culture-distinction]].

## Question and Context

After *Le Cru et le Cuit* established raw/cooked as the culinary axis of South American fire myths, how does structural analysis continue with substances that sit **beside** cuisine — and what does a “mythology of dearth” (dry-season wild products, noise, smoke) reveal about the mind’s combinatorial power?

[[levi-strauss-claude|Lévi-Strauss]]’s second *Mythologiques* volume (Plon 1966/67; English *From Honey to Ashes*, 1973) follows two itineraries that rejoin: **honey** as nature’s seductive, ready-made product; **tobacco** smoke rising toward supernaturals and holding culture’s side during scarcity.

## Design and Methods

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Unit | Myth systems as transformation groups (all versions of a group) |
| Corpus | M-numbered South American myths (Bororo, Gê, Tupi, Chaco, Guiana, Tukuna, etc.) + European metaphor framing |
| Continuity | Bird-nester armature from Vol. I; systems S₁ / S₋₁ / S₂ / S₋₂ / S₃ / S₋₃ |
| Method | Opposition, inversion, mediation; multi-code convertibility (alimentary / astronomical / sociological / acoustic); “spiral” field method |
| Scale | Continental comparative (macro) |

**Program rule (attributed):** themes are given by myths, not speculative hypotheses — the analyst follows a program imposed by the mythical matter. Method maxim restated in the coda: “ce n’est pas la comparaison qui fonde la généralisation, mais le contraire.”

## Findings

*(Attributed to Lévi-Strauss, 1966/67.)*

### Honey and tobacco as entours du repas

- Honey and tobacco are consumable but not properly “cuisine”: honey is elaborated by bees ready to eat (**infra-culinary**); tobacco is incinerated so one inhales smoke (**meta-culinary** / ultra-culinary).
- European “à miel” / “à tabac” locutions form an antithetical couple (abundance vs dearth, sweetness vs violence); the oppositional *relation* can precede the opposed things (medieval *feu de miel*).
- South America is the privileged field: both substances co-present in time and space (North American asymmetry deferred to Vol. III).

### Transformation groups

- Closed systems link origin of cooking fire (S₁), tobacco (S₃), honey feast (S₋₃), etc., often via bird-nester armature with inverted messages (e.g. Bororo culture-goods vs Gê fire; jaguar vs wild pigs).
- Tobacco as **pivot**: smoke/feather series; Chaco tobacco from destructive fire; smoked vs drunk tobacco as internal reproduction of honey/tobacco opposition.

### Narrative cycles

- **La bête aride** / masters of honey (Ofaié, canids, irará, Fox).
- **Fille folle de miel** (Chaco Toba/Matako; Gê bird-killer variants): seduction, hydromel origin, open/closed body, moon/capybara codes.
- **Festin de la grenouille**: combinatorial variations (sex/species inversions; Haburi cycle; wood fiancée; menstrual blood as “honey”).
- **Août en carême**: dry-season mythology; Pleiades–honey–viscera; *ceucy*/Jurupari; forest acoustic codes; return of the bird-nester (Tereno/Tacana).
- **Instruments des ténèbres**: noise and stench as operators; clappers, drums, rattles; European Holy Week / Chinese cold-food parallels; calabash system; culinary-operations diagram.

### Philosophical coda

- Mythical thought reaches a point where it contemplates, beyond images adhering to concrete experience, a world of concepts whose relations are defined freely within the mind’s architecture — analogous to the Greek threshold where mythology yields to philosophy as precondition of science.
- But this is **not progress**: logic of qualities and logic of forms belong to the *same* myths; new materials act as a “developer” (*révélateur*) for latent properties.
- Passage to philosophy/science was **not necessary** among “savages” or among us — nested states of thought do not succeed by ineluctable causality (seed-dormancy analogy).
- Structural analysis does **not** reject history: it grants history first place — irreducible contingency without which necessity could not be conceived; bows before “the power and inanity of the event.”

## Theoretical Impact

- Extends *Mythologiques* from culinary triangle core to infra-/meta-culinary surrounds and acoustic mediation.
- Supplies key materials for later reappraisals of a “second,” more dynamic structuralism (Viveiros de Castro and others — see [[structuralism]], [[levi-strauss-claude]]).
- Reinforces anti-evolutionist reading of mind (linked to *[[levi-strauss-savage-mind-1962|La Pensée sauvage]]*).

## Current Standing

Canonical second volume of the structuralist myth cycle; English *From Honey to Ashes* remains the Anglophone teaching text for honey/tobacco analysis.
