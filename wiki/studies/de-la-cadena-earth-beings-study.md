# Earth Beings (study)

Landmark theoretical ethnography by [[de-la-cadena-marisol|Marisol de la Cadena]] (*Earth Beings: Ecologies of Practice across Andean Worlds*, Duke University Press, 2015; Lewis Henry Morgan Lectures 2011). Source: [[de-la-cadena-earth-beings-2015]]. Population focus: [[pacchanta|Pacchanta]] via Mariano and Nazario Turpo; geographic-relational pivot [[ausangate|Ausangate]].

> **Deep analysis:** [[hubs/studies/de-la-cadena-earth-beings-study|Studies Hub]] (design as conducted, findings with evidence, afterlife).

## Question and Context

How to write ethnography with Andean interlocutors whose practices enroll [[earth-beings|tirakuna]] (e.g. Ausangate) that liberal politics and naturalist science cannot recognize — without reducing those practices to cultural belief about one shared Nature? How did Mariano Turpo’s mid-century land struggle against hacienda Lauramarca and Nazario Turpo’s 2000s tourism/museum “Andean shamanism” enact politics across partially connected worlds?

Intellectual toolkit (attributed): [[partial-connections]] (Strathern); [[controlled-equivocation]] (Viveiros de Castro); [[cosmopolitics]] / divergence (Stengers); “more than one, less than many” (Haraway); Latour’s Modern Constitution + Quijano’s coloniality of power; Rancière on the part of those who have no part.

## Findings

All findings **attributed** to de la Cadena’s co-labored account (documentation 2002–2007; historical narrative deeper).

### Method and worlding (Story 1)

Co-labor ≠ branded collaboration: cancel theory-for-anthropologist / belief-for-local bifurcation. Runakuna participate in modern institutions that cannot know tirakuna; earth-beings are their names (no meaning mediation as cultural “belief”). *Willakuy* (telling) ≠ *historia* ≠ *kwintu*; controlling the history equivocation is part of co-labor. Asymmetric access: Mariano mediates tirakuna; anthropologist can read documents but cannot “know Ausangate” with usual epistemic tools.

### Being-in-ayllu and land struggle (Interlude 1; Stories 2–3)

[[ayllu]] as weaving-like socionatural collective (Justo Oxa); personero speaks *from* ayllu; *wakcha* if separated. Mariano chosen late 1940s–early 1950s; *hatun queja* / walking the grievance through lawyers, FTC, unions, police alliances, caves, torture, Cuzco jail. Hacendado as [[munayniyuq]]; peonage as racial bare life. “Peasant movement” = equivocation making ayllu confrontation audible while earth-beings remain unthinkable. Lawyers **and** Ausangate needed each other; *despachos* as political pago, not only religion. Dual ceremonies (state + earth-beings) at Presidential Palace and reform transfer (*santa tira* / *pukara*).

### Archive and the ahistorical (Story 4)

Mariano’s papers as boundary object and alter-archive; indigenous archivists; Qusñipata 1926 origin narratives; dead leaders still in-ayllu; property vs ayllu translation in buy-Lauramarca campaigns. Extend **eventfulness** (not historicity) to earth-beings — against merely adding nonhumans to Latourian history.

### Multicultural shamanism and museum (Interlude 2; Stories 5–6)

Nazario: abandonment biopolitics; cooperative president 1980 then resignation; tourism replaces wool; *chamán andino* as new subject position (preferred *yachaq*/*hampiq*; refused *altomisayoq*). Toledo 2001 Machu Picchu *despacho* (with Carmona; vs Q’eros narrative). NMAI Quechua exhibit: comedy of equivocations (*suq’a* ≠ ancestors; spirituality slot; picture of Ausangate ≠ Ausangate; hierarchical collaboration vs co-labor). Eliane Karp multicultural misrecognition (Q’ero misID; irrigation letter ignored).

### Munayniyuq and ethnographic cosmopolitics (Story 7; Epilogue)

Ausangate and hacendado as munayniyuq; residual munay in post-reform state (bureaucratic wait, literacy). Rondas campesinas contest present munay without liberal representation. 2006 Sinakara mining demo: environment frame wins (no mine as of 2014 in text); tirakuna withdrawn from public concern. Radical difference relational, not indigenous property. Ethnographic cosmopolitics: slow down; make room for divergence (Stengers); *cosmovivir* / Mariano’s *sumaq kawsay* as uncommons commons vs developmentalist Buen Vivir.

## Theoretical Impact

- Major Andean case for the [[ontological-turn]] beside Amazonian [[perspectivism]] / [[four-ontologies]].
- Densifies [[ayllu]] beyond kin-corporation definitions; supplies political (not only cosmological) stakes.
- Template for [[co-laboring]] and ethnographic [[cosmopolitics]]; museum and tourism studies of indigeneity.
- Links extractive conflict, land reform, and other-than-human politics without collapsing into environmentalism alone.
