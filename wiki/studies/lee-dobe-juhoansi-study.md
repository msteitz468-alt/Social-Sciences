# The Dobe Ju/'hoansi (Lee, 4th ed. 2013) — study summary

Longitudinal teaching ethnography by [[lee-richard-b|Richard B. Lee]] (*[[lee-dobe-juhoansi-2013|source]]*). Complements the research monograph *[[lee-kung-san-1979|The !Kung San]]* / [[lee-kung-san-study]]. No Studies Hub (densifying textbook; hub already exists for 1979 charter).

## Question and Context

*(Attributed — Lee.)* Present the Ju/'hoansi to anthropology students: classic foraging adaptation (1960s–80s) **and** transformation under state, market, and epidemic through 2011 — without freezing an ethnographic present.

## Design and Methods

Multi-decade residence and restudy; synthesis of Harvard Kalahari Project themes in case-study format; Ch.13 original 2010–11 Tsumkwe survey (five domains: subsistence, cash, education, health, religion); App. B oral history for Kalahari Debate; App. A Christmas ox essay.

## Findings

*(Attributed — Lee, *Dobe Ju/'hoansi* 4th ed. 2013.)*

- **Classic foraging package (textbook form of 1960s–80s data):** ~2.4 subsistence days/week; ~2,355 Cal + ~96 g protein/day; plants ~70% / meat ~30%; mongongo staple; n!ore, hxaro, leveling, 22-homicide series — aligned with *[[lee-kung-san-1979|The !Kung San]]* apparatus.
- **Marriage/sexuality:** arranged first marriages, long bride service, high early divorce, relative gender equality by Lee's account; see Ch.6.
- **[[complaint-discourse]]:** Rosenberg chapter on aging, care networks, humorous complaint as reciprocity engine.
- **Change to 2011:** Dobe vs Nyae Nyae divergence (school, TGLP, CBNRM vs Tsumkwe settlement, SADF, 1991 land conference, conservancy); H&G calorie share falling (~85%→~30% in Lee's summary trajectory); HIV Ju estimates ~3–6% vs high national rates.
- **Tsumkwe 2010–11 survey (n=98):** mixed wild/store subsistence; majority cash access; church + traditional religion; TB heavy; bush life still valued (attitude rise vs late 1960s).
- **App. B:** Ju oral history (*n//a k aishe*) as isolationist densification of [[kalahari-debate]]; App. A Christmas ox for [[insulting-the-meat]].

## Theoretical Impact

Primary classroom vehicle for Dobe material after 1984; keeps Ju/'hoan case current past the 1979 monograph moment.

## Critiques, Reanalyses, and Replications

`not-applicable` as single-site longitudinal ethnography; Kalahari Debate positions recorded on [[kalahari-debate]]. Teaching series genre compresses research monograph detail — prefer *The !Kung San* for quantitative apparatus.

## Current Standing

Standard undergrad case study; 4th ed. valued for Tsumkwe survey and updated land/AIDS/politics chapters.
