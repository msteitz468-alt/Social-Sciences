# World Prehistory Timeline

A synthetic chronology of the human past from the earliest hominins to the eve of
European overseas expansion, following the standard teaching narrative of
[[fagan-durrani-people-of-the-earth-2022|Fagan & Durrani, *People of the Earth* (16th ed.)]].
Everything here is **attributed to that textbook's synthesis** — a received, decade-lagging
teaching account, not wiki-voice consensus (see the source page's `reliability_notes`).
Fast-moving areas (modern-human origins, the [[peopling-of-the-americas]], archaeogenetics)
are flagged; consult the linked debate and primary-source pages for current standing.

**Date conventions** (Fagan & Durrani): **BP** = years before present (present = AD 1950),
used before 40,000 BP; **BC/AD** after 12,000 BP; **Ma** = million years ago. Deep-time
dates rest on [[potassium-argon-dating]], [[radiocarbon-dating]] (AMS, calibrated), and
allied methods; carry their uncertainty.
