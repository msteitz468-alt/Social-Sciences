## Volume Synthesis Note

Klinenberg’s social autopsy of July 1995 Chicago heat mortality: weather as trigger; isolation, place-based ecology (North Lawndale vs Little Village), failed city services, PR, and spectacular news as co-producers of who dies. ~739 excess deaths in the peak window; elderly living alone and depleted neighborhoods at highest risk. Portable method label **social autopsy**; 2015 preface climate-scales the case.
