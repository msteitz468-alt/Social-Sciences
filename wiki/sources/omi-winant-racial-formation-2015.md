## Volume Synthesis Note

*Racial Formation* is the charter text of a research program that made **race as process** teachable against ethnicity assimilationism, class reduction, nation essentialism, and biological race science. The third edition re-centers **colorblind racial ideology** as hegemonic [[racial-project]] converging with neoliberalism, reading the post-1970 period as prolonged racial reaction after a democratic “Great Transformation,” with Obama heightening rather than ending colorblind tensions.

**What it adds to this wiki:** (1) [[racial-formation-theory]] + Theory Hub; (2) thinker pages for Omi and Winant; (3) concept kit ([[racial-formation]], [[racial-project]], [[racialization]], [[colorblind-racism]], [[ethnicity-paradigm]]); (4) bridge from Du Bois [[the-color-line]] / [[double-consciousness]] to late-20th-century race theory; (5) engagement with Wilson underclass and Massey-type stratification inside the class-paradigm critique; (6) racial-state politics for [[political-sociology]].
