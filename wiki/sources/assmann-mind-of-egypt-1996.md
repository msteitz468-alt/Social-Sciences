# The Mind of Egypt (Assmann 1996/2002)

Jan Assmann's *Sinngeschichte* of pharaonic Egypt — a history of the meanings, self-images, and "semantic universe" Egyptians built around their own history, from the predynastic period to the rise of Christianity. Organized by period (Old Kingdom, Middle Kingdom, New Kingdom, Third Intermediate/Late, Persian–Greek), each read through three evidential registers Assmann calls **traces, messages, and memories**.
