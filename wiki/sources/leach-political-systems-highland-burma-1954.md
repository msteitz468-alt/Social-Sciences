## Bibliographic Record

E. R. Leach, *Political Systems of Highland Burma: A Study of Kachin Social Structure* (London: London School of Economics and Political Science / G. Bell and Sons, 1954). Reprinted 1964 with a new Introductory Note by the author. Foreword by [[firth-raymond|Raymond Firth]]. Landmark analysis of [[kachin|Kachin]] and [[shan|Shan]] political relations in the Kachin Hills Area (northeast Burma / Myanmar frontier), and a theoretical critique of equilibrium models in British social anthropology.
