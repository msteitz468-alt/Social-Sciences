## Key Claims Extracted

**Split: THESES (Diamond) vs reported FACTS as he states them.** Nothing below is wiki-voice adjudication.

### THESES (quarantine / debate)

1. **One-sentence answer:** history’s pattern of inequality reflects environmental differences among continents, not innate differences among peoples.
2. **Yali’s question** generalizes to why wealth/power (and by 1500 technology/political organization) became distributed as they did.
3. **[[ultimate-and-proximate-causes|Proximate]]** package: guns, germs, steel, ships, states, writing; **ultimate** = biogeography of food production and diffusion.
4. **Farmer power:** food production → denser populations, specialists, stored surplus, epidemic diseases, draft animals → conquest advantages.
5. **[[continental-axis]]:** east–west diffusion faster than north–south.
6. **Anna Karenina principle:** big-mammal domestication fails if any of several criteria fail; few large domesticates, mostly Eurasian.
7. **Lethal gift of livestock:** crowd diseases of Eurasian origin as major demographic weapon in the Americas.
8. **Political evolution:** band–tribe–chiefdom–state with “kleptocracy” and ideology (Service-like ladder).
9. **Epilogue four factors:** (1) domesticable biota + extinctions; (2) intracontinental diffusion rates; (3) intercontinental transfer ease; (4) area/population size.
10. **China vs Europe (Epilogue):** ecological fragility of Fertile Crescent; European political fragmentation vs Chinese unity as post-head-start divergence story.

### FACTS / DESCRIPTIVE (as Diamond reports — secondary, dated)

- Cajamarca 1532 narrative from Spanish eyewitnesses (proximate illustration).
- Lists of independent food-production centers and founder packages (as of his tables).
- Ancient fourteen large domestic mammals; Major Five.
- Virgin-soil epidemic narratives for New World depopulation (figures e.g. high mortality rates are **his** synthesis, not wiki-voice demography).
- Polynesian island environmental variation as comparative setup (Moriori–Maori, etc.).
