# From the Enemy's Point of View (1992 [1986])

Eduardo Viveiros de Castro's ethnography of the Araweté, a Tupi-Guarani people of the eastern Amazon (Ipixuna River, Pará, Brazil), based on fieldwork 1981–83. The ethnographic foundation of his later theoretical program ([[perspectivism]], the "[[cannibal-cogito|cannibal cogito]]", *[[viveiros-de-castro-cannibal-metaphysics-2014|Cannibal Metaphysics]]*).
