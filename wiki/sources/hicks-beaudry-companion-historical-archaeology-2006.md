# The Cambridge Companion to Historical Archaeology (Hicks & Beaudry eds., 2006)

Handbook overview of international historical archaeology (c. 1500 CE–present) in seventeen commissioned essays. Five parts: (I) archaeology and history (documentary archaeology, time, writing); (II) key themes (colonialism, urban, heritage/contemporary past, Marxism and capitalism, industrialisation, maritime); (III) material culture (material culture studies, ceramics); (IV) landscapes (memory; ideology and experience); (V) buildings and households; Cunliffe afterword.
