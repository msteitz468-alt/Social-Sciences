## Core argument (attributed; shared with U.S. text)

Summarised from the already-ingested twin — see [[stringer-lone-survivors-2012]] for the full attributed restatement:

1. **African primacy** of anatomically modern *Homo sapiens*; living non-Africans as a subset of African genetic variation with serial founder decline of diversity with distance from Africa (as of Stringer 2011/2012).
2. **Classic RAO updated, not abandoned** — nuclear aDNA (~2% Neanderthal in non-Africans; Denisovan input in Melanesians) reframed as “mostly Out of Africa” / modest assimilation while still labeled [[recent-african-origin]] when archaic contribution stays modest (as of Stringer 2011/2012).
3. **Anatomy ≠ behaviour; modernity is composite** — MSA symbolism, gradualist vs late-switch accounts; modernity not a single Garden of Eden package (as of Stringer 2011/2012).
4. **Archaic competence revised upward** relative to hard 1990s replacement rhetoric; multi-human Pleistocene (Neanderthals, Denisovans, possibly *H. floresiensis*); sole *sapiens* survival contingent (as of Stringer 2011/2012).
5. **Regional (“racial”) features late** — shared species morphology first; regional traits largely drift with selection exceptions — not deep multiregional *erectus* inheritance (as of Stringer 2011/2012).
6. **Human evolution continues** under agriculture and medicine (as of Stringer 2011/2012).
