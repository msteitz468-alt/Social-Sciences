# Land Behind Baghdad (Adams 1965)

Robert McC. Adams's history of settlement and irrigation on the lower Diyala
plains northeast of Baghdad, from the ʿUbaid period to ca. 1900 CE — the
methodological charter of Mesopotamian regional settlement survey and the
companion-predecessor to [[adams-heartland-of-cities-1981|Heartland of Cities]] (1981).
