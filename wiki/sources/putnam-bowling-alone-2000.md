## Volume Synthesis Note

*Bowling Alone* is the charter U.S. multi-indicator statement of the **civic social-capital research program**. Section I defines [[civic-social-capital]] (networks + reciprocity/trust), popularizes [[bonding-social-capital]]/[[bridging-social-capital]], and states the triangulation method. Section II documents multi-domain [[civic-disengagement]] (politics through trust) with the league-bowling synecdoche. Section III attributes causes — generation ~50%, TV ~25%, sprawl ~10%, time/money ≤~10%, residual unexplained — and rejects family, race/white-flight, and big-government primary stories. Section IV (Goss assist) correlates a 14-indicator Social Capital Index with education, safety, prosperity, health, and democracy. Section V offers Progressive-Era analogy and a 2010 "social capitalists" agenda; Appendices document measurement.

**Relation to already-ingested work:** Tocquevillian [[voluntary-association]] is the deep intellectual ancestor; Granovetter [[strength-of-weak-ties]] is cited for bridging but kept distinct; Bourdieu [[social-capital]] is a **different object** (separate page). Lipset *Political Man* shares survey/democracy ground without the SC apparatus. **Voice:** all trends, causes, and outcomes attributed. **Hub:** Studies Hub built in-session (founded a research program; anchors [[bowling-alone-debate]]). Thinkers Hub deferred (single major U.S. monograph in collection; *Making Democracy Work* not fully ingested).
