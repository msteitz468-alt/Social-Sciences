# Earth Beings

Marisol de la Cadena, *Earth Beings: Ecologies of Practice across Andean Worlds* (Duke University Press, 2015; Lewis Henry Morgan Lectures 2011). Foreword by Robert J. Foster and Daniel R. Reichman. Author: [[de-la-cadena-marisol]]. **Study page:** [[de-la-cadena-earth-beings-study]]. **Studies Hub:** [[hubs/studies/de-la-cadena-earth-beings-study]].

Landmark Andean theoretical ethnography of co-labor with Mariano and Nazario Turpo of [[pacchanta|Pacchanta]] (near [[ausangate|Ausangate]] / Ocongate, Cuzco). Traces indigenous politics from 1950s–60s land struggle through 2000s multicultural tourism and museum collaboration, while arguing that [[earth-beings|tirakuna / earth-beings]] participate in runakuna life and politics in ways modern institutions cannot recognize without reduction. Programmatic contribution to the [[ontological-turn]] via Andean (not only Amazonian) materials; deploys [[partial-connections]], [[controlled-equivocation]], and [[cosmopolitics]] (Stengers).

## Claims Extracted

Principal load-bearing claims (attributed to de la Cadena 2015 / Turpo co-labor unless noted). **317 claims** across 5 ranges (`scratchpad/earth_beings_cache/claims/`).

1. **Tirakuna / earth-beings** (*tierra* + *-kuna*) are other-than-human participants; Apu Ausangate among the most powerful; not cultural beliefs about one Nature (Preface; Story 1).
2. **Co-laboring** cancels anthropologist/informant theory bifurcation; Story 1 replaces introduction; *k’intu* welcomes tirakuna into archive conversations.
3. **Partial connections** (Strathern) and **controlled equivocation** (Viveiros) structure translation gaps (*suerte* refusal; *willakuy*/*historia*/*kwintu*).
4. **Ayllu** as weaving-like socionatural relation (Justo Oxa); personero speaks *from* ayllu; *wakcha* if separated (Interlude 1).
5. **Lauramarca land struggle** (~1904–1969): peonage, gamonalismo, munayniyuq hacendado; Mariano’s networks, torture, jail; reform 6 Dec 1969; “peasant movement” as equivocation (Stories 2–3).
6. **Cosmopolitics**: lawyers and Ausangate need each other; despachos as political pago; coloniality of modern politics (Latour + Quijano) excludes earth-beings from counting (Story 3).
7. **Archive** (600+ docs): eventfulness of the ahistorical; alter-archive; property vs ayllu translation (Story 4).
8. **Nazario**: abandonment biopolitics; cooperative 1980; tourism *chamán andino* (refuses altomisayoq); Toledo 2001 despacho; NMAI comedy of equivocations (*suq’a* ≠ ancestors; spirituality slot; Karp misrecognition) (Interlude 2; Stories 5–6).
9. **Munayniyuq**: Ausangate and hacendado as owners of will; residual munay in state; rondas as partial contest (Story 7).
10. **Epilogue**: 2006 Sinakara mining demo; environment frame wins, tirakuna withdrawn; radical difference relational; ethnographic cosmopolitics (Stengers); *cosmovivir* / *sumaq kawsay*.
