## Volume Synthesis Note

Fraser defends two-dimensional justice (redistribution + recognition) under the standard of **participatory parity**, with recognition as a **status** model and perspectival dualism of class/status. Honneth defends **recognition monism**: distribution struggles are species of recognition conflict within capitalism’s recognition order, grounded in experiences of disrespect. Rejoinders sharpen the dualism-vs-monism and moral-psychology-vs-political-sociology stakes. Primary wiki home of the dispute: [[redistribution-recognition-debate]].
