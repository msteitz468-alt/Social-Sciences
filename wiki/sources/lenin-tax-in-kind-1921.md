# The Tax in Kind and the Trade-Union Debate (CW Vol. 32 selections, 1921)

The NEP-founding cluster: the trade-union controversy (Dec 1920–Jan 1921), the Tenth Congress (March 1921 — tax in kind, Party Unity, the syndicalist-deviation resolution), and the *Tax in Kind* pamphlet. 6-agent extraction over three slices plus one recovery slice.

## Load-bearing content (all attributed)

- **The trade-union controversy:** unions as "a school of communism," transmission belts, "reservoir of the state power"; "not actually a workers' state but a workers' and peasants' state... with a bureaucratic twist"; the dialectics-vs-eclecticism tumbler illustration; Rudzutak's theses; "the Party is sick." Full apparatus on [[soviet-trade-union-debate]].
- **Tenth Congress:** the tax in kind replacing surplus appropriation (self-criticism: over-nationalization "a patent mistake"); the Party Unity resolution dissolving factions (Workers' Opposition, Democratic Centralism) with the unpublished CC-expulsion clause; the syndicalist-anarchist-deviation resolution against the "producer" category; Kronstadt as frame ([[vanguard-party]], [[new-economic-policy]]).
- **The Tax in Kind pamphlet:** the five socio-economic structures (patriarchal, small-commodity, private-capitalist, state-capitalist, socialist); "state capitalism is nothing to fear... a step forward"; the real struggle as petty-bourgeois anarchy vs. state capitalism plus socialism; concessions as state capitalism; the economic roots of bureaucracy in scattered small production ([[state-capitalism]], [[mode-of-production]], [[bureaucracy]]).
- **Concessions doctrine:** the ten CPC principles; Baku/Grozny quarter-leases (off the record at the Congress); "two crutches"; the Transcaucasus letter urging a "slower, more cautious" non-Russian path to socialism.

## Reliability

Primary source for NEP's founding and the party's internal closure of 1921. Trotsky's, Bukharin's, and the Workers' Opposition's texts are present only as Lenin's quotations; Kronstadt appears only in the regime's framing. The Pavlov decree (Jan 1921) is noted in claims as an institutional-history datum.
