## Volume Synthesis Note

*Presentation of Self* is Goffman's founding **dramaturgical handbook**: a formal framework for studying any social establishment as a scene of performance and impression management, illustrated from mixed sources including Shetland hotel/community field materials. The progressive design moves from information and working consensus, through front and individual performance techniques, to teams, regions, discrepant roles, covert communication, and the arts of saving the show — closing with the merchants-of-morality dialectic and dramaturgy as a fifth perspective on establishments.

**Relation to already-ingested work:** Completes the mid-career trilogy with *Asylums* (institutional remaking of the self) and *Stigma* (spoiled identity).
