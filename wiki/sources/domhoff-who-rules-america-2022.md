## Volume Synthesis Note

Domhoff’s 8th edition restates the long-run class-domination program with 2020s party framing: a corporate community intertwined with a social upper class, led through a policy-planning network (Domhoff’s power elite), shapes opinion and elections, and dominates federal government via special-interest and policy-making channels — without conspiracy. Comparative claim: US economic-network ascendancy and class domination can deepen even as individual rights expand. All empirical magnitudes are edition- and citation-dated; treat as attributed synthesis.
