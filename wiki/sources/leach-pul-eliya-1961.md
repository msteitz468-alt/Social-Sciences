## Bibliographic Record

E. R. Leach, *Pul Eliya: A Village in Ceylon. A Study of Land Tenure and Kinship* (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1961). Landmark single-village ethnography of irrigated rice land tenure, kinship, and labour organisation in the Ceylon Dry Zone, and a major empirical critique of British [[descent-theory]] and of treating kinship structure as a self-subsistent “thing.”
