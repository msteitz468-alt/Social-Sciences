## Volume Synthesis Note

STPS is the charter historical-sociological account of the [[bourgeois-public-sphere]]: private people assembled as a public using reason against public authority, grounded in market civil society, intimate sphere, and literary-political institutions — and of that sphere’s **structural transformation** into culture consumption, advertising-driven mass media, manufactured publicity, and welfare-state service citizenship, leaving “public opinion” as constitutional fiction or poll aggregate.
