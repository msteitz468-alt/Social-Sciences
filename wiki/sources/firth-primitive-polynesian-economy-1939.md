# Primitive Polynesian Economy (Firth, 1939)

Raymond Firth's economic monograph on Tikopia, drawn from his 1928–29 fieldwork — commonly credited as the first full-scale ethnographic study of a "primitive" economy framed against formal economic theory, and the founding empirical text of [[economic-anthropology]].
