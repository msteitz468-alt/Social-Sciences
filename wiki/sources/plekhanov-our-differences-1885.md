# Our Differences (1885)

The founding programmatic document of Russian Marxism — the Emancipation of Labour group's declaration of war on Narodnaya Volya, the book Lenin's 1894 polemic treats as the movement's charter, and (per the edition's apparatus) a work Engels praised. ~112k words, 4-agent extraction. With this, the wiki holds Plekhanov's five major works, 1885–1910.

## The argument (attributed)

- **Letter to Lavrov:** the defense of open polemic among revolutionaries; Marxism as "the algebra of the revolution"; "we do not possess a finished programme"; the Emancipation of Labour group's founding purpose.
- **Must Russia pass through capitalism?** The Introduction's frame — with Plekhanov's admission of his own earlier Narodnik position, and Herzen's and Chernyshevsky's commune arguments quoted at length before the historical-materialist critique of both.
- **Capitalism already developing:** Chapter II's empirical case — home-market theory, the putting-out/domestic system across gubernias, the statistical dispute with V.V./Tikhomirov over worker numbers — the direct ancestor of Lenin's 1894/1899 statistical method ([[lenin-what-the-friends-of-the-people-are-1894]]).
- **The commune disintegrating:** money economy, redemption payments, kulaks vs "airy people" — internal class differentiation a decade before Lenin's Voronezh reanalysis ([[peasantry-as-class]]).
- **Against conspiratorial seizure:** Russian Blanquism as Tkachov-derivative ("Blanquist conspiracy + Bakuninist peasant idealization"); commune disintegration "cannot be stopped by any 'seizures of power' by any secret society"; and the **"Peruvian despotism" warning** — a minority-seizure socialism would yield a paternalist-despotic order, not emancipation. The founding statement of the anti-Blanquist position later central to [[smash-or-capture-the-state-debate]] and to Kautsky's 1918 Bonapartism/Jesuitism argument.
- **The program:** the five-thesis Social-Democratic platform and the explicit two-stage perspective (political liberty first, socialist revolution after capitalist development) — the orthodoxy every later Russian dispute (1905 tactics, permanent revolution, April Theses) positioned itself against.

## Reliability

A faction-founding polemic: Narodnaya Volya's positions are Plekhanov's constructions (Tikhomirov's own texts un-ingested). The multi-strata footnotes (1885/1905/Soviet) are dated in extraction. Engels's praise and the workers'-circle reception are the edition apparatus's claims.
