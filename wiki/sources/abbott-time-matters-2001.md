## Volume Synthesis Note

*Time Matters* is a **methodological–theoretical essay collection**, not a single monograph argument written straight through. The autobiographical prologue supplies the intellectual career arc (sequence turn at Rutgers 1981 → order-statistics failure → optimal matching → GLR critique → narrative program → turning points and boundaries). The epilogue restates a single architecture: reverse-engineer standard methods; name [[general-linear-reality|GLR]]; offer four formal alternatives (demographic entity flows, network/structural interdependence under [[harrison-white|White]], **narrative** program, multivocality); insist that explanation includes narrative and description, not only net-effects causalism.

For this wiki the book is a **methodology pillar** alongside [[lieberson-making-it-count-1985|Lieberson 1985]] and [[ragin-comparative-method-1987|Ragin 1987]]: three internal reform programs that refuse both naive variable ontology and anti-formal qualitative triumphalism, by different routes (identification hygiene; set-theoretic configuration; sequence and process). All three feed [[qualitative-quantitative-divide-debate]] without resolving it.
