# What the "Friends of the People" Are and How They Fight the Social-Democrats (1894)

Lenin's first major work (age 24): a polemic against the liberal-Narodnik writers of *Russkoye Bogatstvo* (Mikhailovsky, Krivenko, Yuzhakov), and the founding text of his lifelong method — Marxist theory plus zemstvo-statistical reanalysis. ~68k words, 3-agent extraction from the combined CW file (Vol. 1 segment).

## Load-bearing content (all attributed)

- **Historical materialism codified:** *Capital* transformed the materialist conception of history "from a hypothesis to a scientifically proven proposition"; the Marx–Darwin analogy; the distinction between *material* social relations (formed without passing through consciousness) and *ideological* ones; society's development as "a process of natural history" ([[historical-materialism]], [[base-and-superstructure]]).
- **Dialectics deflated:** the dialectical method is "nothing else than the scientific method in sociology" — the Hegelian triads a removable "lid," not a proof procedure; "the ideal is nothing but the reflection of the material" ([[dialectical-materialism]]).
- **Against subjective sociology:** the dispute with Mikhailovsky's normative method ("sociology must start with some utopia") — the founding Russian round of the objectivity debate in social science.
- **Peasant differentiation demonstrated:** reanalysis of Shcherbina's Voronezh budget data by economic rather than legal categories (income ratios ~10:2:1), handicraft industry as "pure capitalism" in domestic-system form ([[peasantry-as-class]]).
- **The program:** the factory proletariat as "the sole and natural representative of Russia's entire working and exploited population"; the closing forecast of the Russian worker leading "the victorious COMMUNIST REVOLUTION" — 1894's anticipation of 1917, quoted verbatim on [[class-struggle]]-adjacent pages.
- **Marx's 1877 letter on Russia:** Lenin's exposure of Krivenko's truncated quotation — a primary document in the "Russia's special path" dispute.

## Reliability

A first-rate primary source for the founding era of Russian Marxism and the Narodnik controversy; the Narodniks' positions are known here only through Lenin's hostile quotation. The statistical reanalyses are Lenin's own and were themselves contested — treat the numbers as his claims, not zemstvo ground truth.

The statistical-empirical program sketched here in miniature (Voronezh budget reanalysis by economic strength rather than legal/allotment category) is the direct precursor to the full-scale, multi-gubernia execution Lenin carried out five years later in **[[lenin-development-of-capitalism-in-russia-1899|The Development of Capitalism in Russia]]** (1899, now ingested in full) — the same classification method, generalized across dozens of Zemstvo datasets, anchors that book's differentiation-of-the-peasantry thesis.
