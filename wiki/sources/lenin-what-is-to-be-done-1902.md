## Core argument (attributed)

Lenin argued that:

1. “**Freedom of criticism**” in contemporary Social-Democracy means freedom for an **opportunist** tendency — Bernstein’s reformism and Millerand’s ministerialism — not scientific advance; a revolutionary party is a compact group under fire, free to leave the “marsh” but not to drag the party into it.
2. “Without a revolutionary **theory** there can be no revolutionary movement.” Engels’s three forms of struggle (theoretical, political, economic) all matter; theory is especially critical for Russian Social-Democracy (youth, national tasks, international significance).
3. The working class, exclusively by its own effort, develops only **[[trade-union-consciousness|trade-union consciousness]]** (unions, strikes, labor legislation). **[[consciousness-from-without|Socialist consciousness]]** is brought **from without** — a thesis Lenin anchors with a long **Kautsky** quotation (socialism and class struggle arise side by side; science is carried by the bourgeois intelligentsia and communicated to the proletariat).
4. **[[economism|Economism]]** (as in the *Credo*, *Rabochaya Mysl*, and *Rabocheye Dyelo* as Lenin reads them) bows to **spontaneity**, confining politics to the economic struggle and treating leadership as an intellectual sideline — yielding reformism and *khvostism* (“tail-ism”).
5. Social-Democratic politics requires **all-sided political exposure** of the autocracy, not trade-union secretaryship alone; the ideal is the **tribune of the people**.
6. Against “**primitiveness**” (*kustar* methods): under autocracy the decisive force is an organization of **[[professional-revolutionary|professional revolutionaries]]** (workers and intellectuals), with secrecy, selection, and training — not “broad democratic” toy forms that help the police. Local newspapers and funds are subordinate to an **all-Russian** organization and press (detailed newspaper plan = missing Sec. V).
7. Summary answer: **“Liquidate the Third Period”** of Russian Social-Democracy (the period of diffusion and Economism) and build the professional, centralized movement.
