## Contents extracted (all attributed)

- **Sadovsky & Kelle foreword:** full biographic spine (Malinovsky → Bogdanov; RSDLP CC; Capri/*Vpered*; Proletcult; Communist Academy; Blood Transfusion Institute; death 7 Apr 1928); publication history of *Tektologia*; Soviet silence → 1960s–70s rehabilitation; first complete English claim for this edition.
- **Dudley editor intro:** systems-science reading frame; conservative/progressive selection; world ingression; explicit caution about systemist interpretive bias; project history (Gorelik → Gasparski → Sadovsky → Hull).
- **Author's prefaces:** 1912/13 founding responsibility claim; 1921 reconstruction context; 1923 German preface with Plenge priority note, Petrovitch, Einstein monism, Russian reception.
- **Chs. 1–4:** full formative/regulative/stability architecture (conjugation → law of the leasts → equilibrium systems); mathematics as tektology of neutral complexes; philosophy to be made unnecessary; prototypes (speech, Hegel, Spencer, Marx, Haeckel name).
- **Addenda:** organizational equilibrium of labour/needs; Bazarov distribution norm; uniform economic plan (chain connexions, proportionality, leasts); point-by-point reply to Nevsky, Kerzhentsev, Wainstain.
