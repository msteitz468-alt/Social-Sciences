# Ethnography Through Thick and Thin (Marcus, 1998)

George Marcus's collected essays tracing the evolution of the **[[multi-sited-ethnography|multi-sited ethnography]]** program out of the [[writing-culture|Writing Culture]] moment — from "Imagining the Whole" (1989) through the canonical "Ethnography in/of the World System" (1995, *Annual Review of Anthropology*) to "The Uses of [[complicity]]" (1997) — plus parallel empirical traces (Tongan elites, dynastic wealth, eccentricity) and essays on reflexivity, cultural studies, and the discipline's professional culture.
