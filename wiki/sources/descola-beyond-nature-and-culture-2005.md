## Claims Extracted


Principal load-bearing claims (from 8-range extraction, ~536 claims total):

1. **Nature/culture dualism is local to naturalism**, not a human universal (Preface; Ch.3 Great Divide genealogy).
2. **Identification** schematizes experience via continuity/discontinuity of **interiority** and **physicality** (Ch.5); four combinatorial ontologies (Fig. 1).
3. **Animism** = shared interiority + discontinuous physicality (form/ethogram); culture extended to nonhumans; Achuar base (Ch.1, 6).
4. **Perspectivism** is an optional crossed-inversion graft on animism, not coextensive with all animism (Ch.6).
5. **Totemism (ontological)** = continuity of both axes within hybrid classes; Australia exemplary; vs Lévi-Strauss classificatory dissolution (Ch.7).
6. **Naturalism** = reverse of animism; generates nature/culture; internal heterodoxies (ethology, rights) do not yet exit the mode (Ch.8).
7. **Analogism** = double discontinuity recomposed by analogy, hierarchy, sacrifice; Mexico, West Africa, chain of being (Ch.9).
8. **Collectives** include nonhumans; four morphologies match four ontologies (Ch.11).
9. **Modes of relation** (give/take/exchange/produce/protect/transmit) cross-cut identification; Amazonian predation/exchange/gift triad under one animism (Ch.13–14).
10. **Histories of structures** (Caribou-Man→Lord Bull; taming ≠ protodomestication); structure-before-genesis as method (Ch.15); Epilogue refuses political export of non-Western ontologies.
