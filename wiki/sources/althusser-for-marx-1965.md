## Volume synthesis

*For Marx* is the philosophical charter of [[structural-marxism]]: an interventionist rereading of Marx against post-1956 humanist revival and against Hegelian expressive totality. Load-bearing moves (all attributed): [[epistemological-break]] (~1845); [[overdetermination]] of contradiction; [[structure-in-dominance]] / determination last instance with relative autonomy of superstructures; [[theoretical-practice]] (Generalities I–II–III); [[theoretical-anti-humanism]]; [[social-formation]] as object of [[historical-materialism]]; [[problematic]] as unit of reading. The book is **not** the ISA essay and does not ground interpellation. Anthropological structural Marxism (Godelier/Terray) and *Reading Capital* remain needed for the full program; Sahlins’s critique is already on the theory page.
