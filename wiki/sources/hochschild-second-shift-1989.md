## Volume Synthesis Note

*The Second Shift* is a hybrid theoretical-empirical program that founded the research vocabulary of the **[[second-shift|second shift]]** and the **[[stalled-revolution|stalled revolution]]**. Framework chapters define [[leisure-gap]], [[gender-strategy]] (traditional/transitional/egalitarian), [[family-myth]], and [[economy-of-gratitude]]; ten couple portraits show mechanisms under dual earning; analytic chapters map women's and men's strategies, pathways to sharing men (showdown vs natural drift), and marital tension types; Appendix synthesizes time-use literature and reports sample composition (N=145; 50 couples; middle-class skew).

**Relation to already-ingested work:** domestic companion to *[[hochschild-managed-heart-1983|The Managed Heart]]* — same feeling-rules / emotion-work toolkit applied to unpaid care rather than commercial service; extends [[family]] institution pages beyond classical evolutionary and Middletown cases; [[participant-observation]] exemplary application. **Voice:** theoretical claims and single-study findings attributed throughout. **Hub:** Studies Hub built in-session (selection criteria met: entered the language; organized work–family research). Thinkers Hub still deferred (two monographs without full biography).
