## Volume Synthesis Note

Latour’s systematic ANT intro: redefine the social as re-association (sociology of associations vs sociology of the social); five uncertainties for deploying controversies; mediators vs intermediaries; keep the social flat via three moves; reassemble a *collective* rather than invoke Society. Continuity with *Laboratory Life* black-boxing/inscription, generalized beyond the lab. **FLAG:** Latour’s “collective” ≠ Descola’s collectives-including-nonhumans.
