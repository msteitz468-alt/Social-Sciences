## Volume Synthesis Note

Lareau's ethnography of classed childrearing: middle-class **concerted cultivation** vs working-class/poor **accomplishment of natural growth**. Class structures schedules, language, and institutional interventions; middle-class children often develop entitlement, working-class/poor children more constraint in school-like settings. Race intersects; decade-later follow-up shows largely sustained class trajectories. Bourdieuian capital/habitus apparatus in appendices.
