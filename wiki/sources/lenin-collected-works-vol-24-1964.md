# Lenin, Collected Works Vol. 24 (April–June 1917)

Lenin's writings from his return to Russia (April 3, 1917 O.S.) to early June: the **April Theses**, "The Dual Power," "Letters on Tactics," the Draft Platform (*The Tasks of the Proletariat in Our Revolution*), *Political Parties in Russia* (the A/B/C/D catechism), the Petrograd City and Seventh (April) All-Russia Conferences of the R.S.D.L.P.(B.), the "War and Revolution" lecture, agrarian and programme-revision materials, and a dense run of Pravda polemics. Body ~145k words, 7-agent extraction (front matter through the NOTES boundary).

## Load-bearing content (all attributed)

- **April Theses:** two-stage transition; no support for the Provisional Government; republic of Soviets, not a parliamentary republic; abolition of police/standing army/bureaucracy; land nationalisation; single national bank; "Communist Party" renaming; a new International.
- **[[dual-power]]:** coined and elaborated across "The Dual Power," the Draft Platform, "The 'Crisis of Power'," and "Has Dual Power Disappeared?" — the concept page carries the full apparatus.
- **The Commune-state:** Soviets as "a new type of state," "no longer a state in the proper sense"; the April Conference dispute with Kamenev and Rykov; "we are not Blancists" ([[the-state]], [[smash-the-state-machine]], [[marxist-theory-of-the-state]]).
- **Agrarian analysis:** nationalisation as a bourgeois measure; internal class differentiation of the peasantry; separate organization of agricultural labourers ([[peasantry-as-class]]).
- **Imperialism:** "monopoly has evolved into state monopoly"; programme-revision definition of imperialism as "the epoch of finance capital"; "moribund capitalism, dying but not dead" ([[lenin-theory-of-imperialism]], [[finance-capital]]).
- **Polemical record:** the three-trends taxonomy of wartime socialism; sustained attacks on [[plekhanov-georgi]] and [[kautsky-karl]]; the explicit 1917 characterization of "Trotskyism" ("No tsar, but a workers' government — this is wrong"), recorded on [[permanent-revolution]] and [[trotsky-leon]].

## Reliability

Primary source of the highest value for the 1917 revolution's ideological history; every claim is a combatant's claim, written day-by-day for tactical effect. Conference minutes are reconstructions first printed years later. The Soviet apparatus should not be cited as evidence about the texts' 1917 reception.
