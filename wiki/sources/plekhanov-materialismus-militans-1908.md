# Materialismus Militans (1908–10)

Plekhanov's own anti-Machist polemic — the last missing primary voice of the [[empirio-criticism-debate]], and the third contemporaneous principal (with Lenin's M&E and Bogdanov's *Empiriomonism*) now in the wiki's hands. ~44k words, 2-agent extraction.

## Plekhanov's line (attributed)

- **The exclusion move:** Bogdanov is "outside Marxism" by definition — Marxism rests on dialectical materialism, Bogdanov is a Machist; Plekhanov pointedly refuses him the title "comrade" throughout. Polemically harsher than Lenin's treatment in kind, and grounded in Plekhanov's claim to be defending Engels's exact positions (heavy verbatim marshalling of "Über historischen Materialismus," the Feuerbach notes, the 1874 anti-Blanquist article) against Bogdanov's "Holbachian" charge.
- **The hieroglyph doctrine defended:** sensations as signs (not copies) of things-in-themselves, traced to Sechenov — with Plekhanov's own 1905/06 self-correction acknowledging the terminology as "ambiguous." This is the doctrine Lenin's M&E criticized; the edition's own footnote 65 records Lenin's "an obvious mistake" verdict, giving the wiki dual-provenance documentation that the two chief anti-Machists held *different* epistemologies ([[reflection-theory]]).
- **Against Mach:** self-contradictory oscillation between materialist naturalist and idealist philosopher, sociologically explained by bourgeois hostility to materialism.
- **Against Bogdanov's objectivity-as-socially-organized-experience:** the reductio via collectively believed hobgoblins and witch trials — social coordination cannot constitute objectivity; plus the "five torturing questions" on environment and circularity, and the solipsism and space/time critiques of substitution.
- **On Bogdanov's independence claim:** Letter Three takes up Bogdanov's insistence on distance from Mach (which the wiki now also holds first-hand in [[bogdanov-empiriomonism-1906]]) and rejects it — the three principals' texts now triangulate on exactly this contested point.

## Reliability

A three-way polemic's third corner, in a Soviet edition whose footnotes side with Lenin against the author — the apparatus is drift evidence of the same kind as the Monist View and Riazanov layers. Bogdanov's Open Letter itself (the provocation) remains un-ingested; his positions here are Plekhanov's quotations, checkable against [[bogdanov-empiriomonism-1906]].
