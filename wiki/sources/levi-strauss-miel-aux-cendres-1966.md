# Du miel aux cendres (Lévi-Strauss, Mythologiques II) — Source

## Bibliographic Record

Claude Lévi-Strauss, *Mythologiques. Du miel aux cendres* (Paris: Plon, 1966/1967). Second of four *Mythologiques* volumes (I *Le Cru et le Cuit* 1964; III *L'Origine des manières de table* 1968; IV *L'Homme nu* 1971). Structural analysis of South American myths organized around the honey/tobacco opposition and a “mythology of dearth” (dry-season wild products, instruments of darkness).
