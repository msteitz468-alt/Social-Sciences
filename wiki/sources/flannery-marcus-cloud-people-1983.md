# The Cloud People (Flannery & Marcus, eds., 1983)

Edited seminar volume treating the [[zapotec-civilization|Zapotec]] and [[mixtec-culture|Mixtec]] as a case of **divergent evolution** from a common Otomanguean ancestor — 100 numbered "topics" spanning the Oaxaca preceramic through the Spanish conquest, combining excavation, full-coverage settlement survey, epigraphy, and ethnohistory. Dedicated to [[bernal-ignacio|Ignacio Bernal]].
