# "Left-Wing" Communism—An Infantile Disorder (1920)

Lenin's polemic against the "Left" currents of the young Communist movement (German KAPD tendency, Dutch Tribunists, Bordiga's abstentionists, British anti-parliamentarians like Sylvia Pankhurst and Gallacher), written for the Second Congress of the Communist International. ~30k words plus appendix; 2-agent extraction from the combined Collected Works file (Vol. 31 segment).

## Load-bearing content (all attributed)

- **Retrospective of Bolshevism:** periodization 1903–1920; iron discipline resting on three conditions (vanguard devotion, links with the masses, correctness of leadership verified by mass experience); the boycott errors of 1906–14 and the 1918 Left-Communist episode as instructive mistakes ([[vanguard-party]]).
- **Compromise doctrine:** the Brest-Litovsk "bandit" analogy; Engels's 1874 anti-Blanquist polemic quoted; the Bolsheviks' own history of blocs (Struve-era *Iskra*, 1907 SR bloc, Zimmerwald) as evidence that "no compromises" is "childishness."
- **Work in reactionary institutions:** trade unions ("that is where the masses are"), bourgeois parliaments (parliamentarianism "historically obsolete" but not "politically obsolete"); the proposed Labour Party electoral compromise in Britain.
- **The "fundamental law of revolution":** the exploited "cannot live in the old way" and the exploiters "cannot rule in the old way"; winning the vanguard ideologically is insufficient — the masses need their own political experience ([[class-consciousness]], [[social-revolution]]).
- **[[dictatorship-of-the-proletariat]]:** "a most determined and most ruthless war," with small-scale production "engendering capitalism... daily, hourly, spontaneously"; the party/class/leaders relation against the German Lefts' dichotomy.

## Reliability

Primary source for Comintern-era communist strategy and for Bolshevism's self-presentation in 1920. Membership figures, vote counts, and press citations are Lenin's assertions from partisan sources; the historical narrative is victors' history written by the victor.
