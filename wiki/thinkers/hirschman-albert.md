# Albert O. Hirschman

## Overview

Albert O. Hirschman (1915–2012) was a German-born American economist and social theorist whose work spanned development economics, the history of political ideas, and the analysis of organizational and political response to decline. This wiki's primary ingest is *[[hirschman-exit-voice-loyalty-1970|Exit, Voice, and Loyalty]]* (1970), written 1968–69 at the Center for Advanced Study in the Behavioral Sciences (Stanford), which supplies the [[exit-voice-loyalty]] triad used across political science, organizational sociology, and economics. Dedicated to Eugenio Colorni (1909–1944), "who taught me about small ideas and how they may grow."

## Contributions

*(Attributed from EVL unless noted.)*

- **[[exit-voice-loyalty]] framework.** Repairable lapses and continuous [[organizational-slack|slack]]; dual recuperation via [[exit-option|exit]] and [[voice-option|voice]]; [[loyalty]] as hold-and-activate mechanism; public-goods residual exit; elusive optimal mix.
- **Interdisciplinary reciprocity.** Exit and voice as "two principal actors of strictly equal rank"; critique of one-way colonization of political science by scarcity/allocation tools; market and nonmarket forces jointly, not Manichaean laissez-faire vs intervention.
- **Prior development work (self-cited).** *Strategy of Economic Development* (1958): development as calling forth hidden/badly utilized resources; pressure mechanisms squeeze slack. *Development Projects Observed* (1967): Nigerian railways paragraph that sparked EVL. "Hiding Hand" underestimates that push people into creativity-requiring courses (invoked for loyalty's function).
- **Formal toolkit (appendices).** Quality-demand diagrams; certain-exit vs lottery-voice; reversal phenomenon for connoisseur goods; initiation×exit-cost experiment design with Zimbardo & Snyder.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

EVL is self-consciously short and expansive; Hirschman worried exit and voice "might be too broad." Specific cases (Nigeria, Vietnam non-resignation) are period-bound illustrations. Relation to [[collective-action-theory]] is adjacency rather than synthesis — voice-as-public-good free-riding is noted, not solved. Later reception and *Passions and the Interests* / *Shifting Involvements* not yet ingested.
