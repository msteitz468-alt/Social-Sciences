# Michel-Rolph Trouillot

Michel-Rolph Trouillot (1949–2012) was a Haitian anthropologist and historian of the Caribbean, known for analyses of power in historical production. Seeded here from "The North Atlantic Universals" in *[[wallerstein-modern-world-system-longue-duree-2004|The Modern World-System in the Longue Durée]]* (2004).

## Overview

Trouillot proposes [[north-atlantic-universals|North Atlantic universals]] as a way to parochialize concepts that present North Atlantic experience as world-standard. Full monographs not yet ingested.

## Contributions

- [[north-atlantic-universals]] — substantive vs classificatory universals; West as project (with Glissant).

Caribbean historical anthropology; dialogue with world-systems and postcolonial critique.
