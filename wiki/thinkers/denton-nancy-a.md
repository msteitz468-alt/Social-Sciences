# Nancy A. Denton

## Overview

American sociologist and demographer; co-author with [[massey-douglas-s|Douglas S. Massey]] of *[[massey-denton-american-apartheid-1993|American Apartheid]]* (1993). Co-developer of the multi-dimensional [[hypersegregation]] measurement program and co-analyst of 1980-census black–white segregation patterns across U.S. metropolitan areas.

## Contributions

- Co-authored the five-dimension segregation framework and hypersegregation cutpoint analysis (*Demography* 1989; book Ch 3).
- Collaborative research on racial/ethnic segregation by SES and generation; housing-market discrimination evidence in the book.
- Study analysis: [[massey-denton-american-apartheid-study]].

## Intellectual Genealogy

Collaborator: [[massey-douglas-s]]. New Faculty Development Award (NYS United University Professors) acknowledged in the preface for support during the project period.
