# Milovan Djilas

## Overview

Yugoslav communist leader turned dissident (1911–1995). In this collection, present primarily as the author of *The New Class* (1957), which Mawdsley and White (2000) list among competing labels for the Soviet ruling group ([[new-class]], alongside Trotsky's bureaucracy and Voslensky's [[nomenklatura]]).

## Contributions

Canonical popularisation of the **[[new-class]]** thesis: communist systems generate a ruling stratum based on political monopoly over nationalised property.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Yugoslav party leadership → imprisonment and critique of the system he helped build; interlocutor for Western Sovietology and later nomenklatura literature.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Class vs elite/status-group disputes; operational boundary problems. Recorded as a **position** in [[mawdsley-white-soviet-elite-study]], not adopted as their measure.
