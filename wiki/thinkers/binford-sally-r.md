# Sally R. Binford

American archaeologist; co-editor with [[binford-lewis|Lewis R. Binford]] of *[[binford-binford-new-perspectives-1968|New Perspectives in Archeology]]* (1968) and contributor of a Near Eastern Mousterian variability paper in that volume.

## Overview

Co-organizer of the 1965 AAA symposium that became *New Perspectives*. Her chapter treats variability and change in the Near Eastern Mousterian of Levallois facies as a problem for functional/systemic interpretation rather than pure culture-historical typology (attributed, *New Perspectives* ch. 4). Distinct person from Lewis R. Binford; do not merge pages.

## Contributions

*(From *New Perspectives* 1968 — attributed.)*

- Co-editor (with [[binford-lewis]]) of the 1968 charter volume; co-organizer of the 1965 AAA symposium.
- **Near Eastern Mousterian of Levallois facies:** treats assemblage variability as functional/tool-kit and adaptive problem, not only culture-historical *fossile directeur* sequencing (attributed, ch. 4).

## Intellectual Genealogy

Collaborator and co-editor with [[binford-lewis]]; part of the early processual generation.
