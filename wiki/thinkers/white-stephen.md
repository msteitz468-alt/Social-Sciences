# Stephen White

## Overview

Political scientist (b. 1945), specialist on Soviet and post-Soviet politics; co-author with [[mawdsley-evan|Evan Mawdsley]] of *The Soviet Elite from Lenin to Gorbachev* (2000). Co-authored methodological work on interviewing the Soviet elite (White, Kryshtanovskaia, Kukolev, Mawdsley, Saldin, 1996).

## Contributions

Elite interviews and long-run CC collective biography; analysis of privilege, representativeness, and late-Soviet elite crisis.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Glasgow/UK Soviet politics tradition; collaboration with Russian Academy of Sciences sociologists on the Soviet Elite Project.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

As for the joint study — boundary choices and post-communist endurance claims.
