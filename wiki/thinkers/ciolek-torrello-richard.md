# Richard Ciolek-Torrello

## Overview

Southwest archaeologist; co-editor of *[[whittlesey-vanishing-river-1997|Vanishing River]]* and principal author of LVAP settlement and demography (Ch. 14), coauthor of site structure (Ch. 13), and major voice in the research-design synthesis (Ch. 18).

## Contributions

- Prehistoric settlement patterns and population estimates for the lower Verde.
- Domestic organization / courtyard groups (with Klucas and Riggs).
- Land-use typology and social-organization discussion in Ch. 18.

## Intellectual Genealogy

SRI / LVAP team with [[whittlesey-stephanie-m|Whittlesey]] and [[altschul-jeffrey-h|Altschul]].

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Population and carrying-capacity figures are model-dependent (see Van West & Altschul Ch. 9) — keep attributed.
