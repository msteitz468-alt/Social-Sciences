# Harrison C. White

American structural sociologist; central figure in the Harvard network-analysis tradition. Appears in this wiki first as the acknowledged intellectual origin of [[granovetter-mark-s|Mark Granovetter]]'s *[[granovetter-strength-of-weak-ties-1973|The Strength of Weak Ties]]* (1973): "This paper originated in discussions with Harrison White, to whom I am indebted for many suggestions and ideas."
