# Loa P. Traxler

## Overview

Research scientist in the American Section of the University of Pennsylvania Museum of Archaeology and Anthropology (as credited in [[sharer-traxler-ancient-maya-2006|Sharer & Traxler, *The Ancient Maya*, 6th ed. 2006]]). Co-author with [[sharer-robert-j|Robert J. Sharer]] of the thoroughly rewritten sixth edition of the standard comprehensive Maya reference.

## Contributions

- Co-authorship of the 2006 sixth edition of *The Ancient Maya*, expanding and rewriting the Sharer fourth/fifth-edition synthesis with then-current archaeology and historical studies (as presented in the book’s front matter).

## Intellectual Genealogy

- Collaborator with Sharer at the University of Pennsylvania Museum; edition sits in the Morley → Brainerd → Sharer reference lineage.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Biographical and research depth beyond the 2006 credit line is not developed in this ingest; treat *Ancient Maya* claims as jointly attributed to Sharer & Traxler (2006).
