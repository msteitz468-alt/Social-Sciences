# Henry T. Wright

## Overview

American archaeologist associated with early-state and settlement-system research. Contributed the appendix **"The Southern Margins of Sumer: Archaeological Survey of the Area of Eridu and Ur"** to [[adams-robert-mcc|Robert McC. Adams]]'s *[[adams-heartland-of-cities-1981|Heartland of Cities]]* (1981), extending Babylonian survey coverage to the Eridu–Ur region.

## Contributions

- Southern Sumer survey complementary to Adams's central floodplain intensive survey.
- Broader program (with Johnson and others) on early state formation and settlement systems — densify as those primaries are ingested.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Peer and collaborator network around University of Michigan / Near Eastern archaeology and Adams's Oriental Institute survey program (details incomplete on this ingest).
