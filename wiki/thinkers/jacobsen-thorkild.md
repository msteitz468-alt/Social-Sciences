# Thorkild Jacobsen

## Overview

Danish-American Assyriologist and historian of ancient Mesopotamia (1904–1993). Wrote the **foreword** to [[adams-robert-mcc|Adams]]'s *[[adams-heartland-of-cities-1981|Heartland of Cities]]* (1981), framing regional survey as equal or superior in importance to spectacular tomb archaeology and linking early cities to "central place" economic-religious roles.

## Contributions

- Assyriological interpretation of Sumerian political and religious institutions (specialist corpus beyond this ingest).
- Methodological endorsement of Adams's ceramic-surface and air-photo survey program; notes Lagash area still unsurveyed as of 1981 foreword.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Oriental Institute and Mesopotamian studies networks; interlocutor with Adams's anthropological archaeology.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Philological reconstructions continually revised; foreword claims are dated to the 1981 frame.
