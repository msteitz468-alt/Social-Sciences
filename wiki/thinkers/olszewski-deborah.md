# Deborah I. Olszewski

American archaeologist; University of Pennsylvania. Co-author of the 5th edition of *[[wenke-olszewski-patterns-in-prehistory-2007|Patterns in Prehistory]]* with [[wenke-robert|Robert J. Wenke]] (OUP 2007). The edition preface credits her research experience in the Middle East, the American Southwest, and earlier periods of prehistory, plus teaching experience with prior editions of the book, as inputs to the reorganization and updates.

## Overview

Olszewski's role in this vault is primarily as **co-author of a major world-prehistory textbook**: standardized chapter structure for complex-society case studies, expanded illustration/timeline apparatus, and revised treatments of early prehistory and New World complexity (attributed, Wenke & Olszewski 2007 preface). Primary research monographs are not yet ingested.
