# Bambi B. Schieffelin

## Overview

American linguistic anthropologist (NYU); major figure in language socialization and in the editorial consolidation of the [[language-ideology]] program. In [[kroskrity-regimes-of-language-2000|*Regimes of Language*]], contributes the long case study of **[[kaluli|Kaluli]] literacy** under Australian fundamentalist mission influence.

## Contributions

- Literacy as contact of language ideologies (mission vs. Kaluli); chronology of texts and practices (*Regimes* ch. 8).
- Co-editor of *Language Ideologies* (1998) with Woolard and Kroskrity.
- Language socialization research tradition (densify from monographs).

## Intellectual Genealogy
