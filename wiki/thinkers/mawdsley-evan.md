# Evan Mawdsley

## Overview

British historian/political scientist (b. 1945), co-author with [[white-stephen|Stephen White]] of the full-period collective biography of the CPSU Central Committee elite ([[mawdsley-white-soviet-elite-study]]).

## Contributions

Long-run prosopography of the Soviet political elite; application of Daniels's [[job-slot-system]] across 1917–1991; integration of archives and elite interviews.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Soviet studies / modern Russian history tradition; collaboration with White and Institute of Sociology (RAS) interview partners.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Scope limited to CC tier (not full nomenklatura lists); later transition studies may revise post-1991 continuity chapter.
