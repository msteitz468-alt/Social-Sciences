# Jeffrey H. Altschul

## Overview

American archaeologist associated with Statistical Research, Inc.; co-editor of *[[whittlesey-vanishing-river-1997|Vanishing River]]* and author of the LVAP chapter on **big projects and cultural resource management** (Ch. 17), coauthor of agricultural economics modeling (Ch. 9 with Van West), and coauthor of the Epilogue.

## Contributions

- Intellectual history and management analysis of LVAP as a “big CRM project.”
- Agricultural productivity / carrying-capacity modeling (with Carla R. Van West).

## Intellectual Genealogy

SRI contract archaeology; collaboration with [[whittlesey-stephanie-m|Whittlesey]] and [[ciolek-torrello-richard|Ciolek-Torrello]].
