# Sidney Verba

## Overview

Sidney Verba (1932–2019) was an American political scientist at Harvard, a leading figure in comparative politics and political behavior (*The Civic Culture*, with Almond) and in the cross-national study of participation, and co-author of *[[king-keohane-verba-designing-social-inquiry-1994|Designing Social Inquiry]]* (1994).

## Contributions

Verba's long career in comparative [[survey-research]] and political behavior informs the KKV emphasis on measurement and [[descriptive-inference]]. In *Designing Social Inquiry* he joined [[king-gary]] and [[keohane-robert]] in arguing for a unified logic of inference across research styles.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Co-authored *Designing Social Inquiry* with [[king-gary]] and [[keohane-robert]].
