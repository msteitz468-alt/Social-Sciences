# Robert V. Daniels

## Overview

American historian/political scientist of the Soviet system; in this collection, present primarily as the formulator of the **[[job-slot-system]]** and **[[circular-flow-of-power]]** analyses of CPSU leadership selection, as adopted and extended by [[mawdsley-evan|Mawdsley]] and [[white-stephen|White]] (2000).

## Contributions

- **Job-slot system:** organic link between ranked offices and Central Committee status of incumbents.
- **Circular flow of power:** apparatus control → regional appointments → congresses → CC → executive organs.
- Leadership-selection studies covering the early Soviet period (as cited).

## Intellectual Genealogy

Western Sovietology; interlocutor for later full-period prosopography ([[mawdsley-white-soviet-elite-study]]).

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Mawdsley and White accept the job-slot and circular-flow toolkit while refining timing (job-slots significant earlier than Daniels's late-1920s maturity date) and embedding them in a four-generation, full-era dataset.
