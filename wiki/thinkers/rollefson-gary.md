# Gary O. Rollefson

Archaeologist principal excavator of [[ain-ghazal|‘Ain Ghazal]] (Jordan), a major Pre-Pottery Neolithic settlement famous for plaster statues, temples, and the mid-Holocene occupation trajectory Mithen narrates as “Town of Ghosts.” Stub from [[mithen-after-the-ice-2003|Mithen 2003]].

## Overview

From 1983, Rollefson’s project documented large-scale PPN architecture, ritual installations, and later pastoral use of the mound ruins (attributed, Mithen 2003).

## Contributions

- Field definition of ‘Ain Ghazal’s sequence and monumental/ritual assemblage for the southern Levantine PPN.
