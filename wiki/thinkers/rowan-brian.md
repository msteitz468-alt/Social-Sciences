# Brian Rowan

## Overview

Brian Rowan co-authored, with [[meyer-john-w]], "Institutionalized Organizations: Formal
Structure as Myth and Ceremony" (1977), reprinted as ch. 2 of *The New Institutionalism
in Organizational Analysis* (1991). DiMaggio and Powell (ch. 1) treat this paper,
alongside Meyer's companion 1977 article on education as an institution, as
new-institutionalism's conventional founding text.

## Contributions

The full theoretical content of Meyer and Rowan's chapter — the [[rationalized-myth]]
concept, the six formal propositions, and the origin of [[decoupling]] and the "logic of
confidence" — is documented on [[meyer-john-w]], the joint chapter's fuller entry. The
ingested source does not distinguish Rowan's individual contribution from Meyer's within
the co-authored text; the collection did not supply biographical material specific to
