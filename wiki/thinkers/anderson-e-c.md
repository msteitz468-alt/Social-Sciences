# E. C. Anderson

Physical chemist and collaborator of **[[libby-willard-f|Willard F. Libby]]** in the development of **[[radiocarbon-dating]]**. Named with **[[arnold-james-r|James R. Arnold]]** in Libby's first-edition preface as essential to the method's development (attributed, Libby 1952 preface). Co-author of early *Science* papers on natural radiocarbon.

## Overview

Anderson's role in *[[libby-radiocarbon-dating-1952|Radiocarbon Dating]]* is as Chicago collaborator on the physical detection of natural C-14 and the early dating program. This page is a network stub from the Libby monograph; biographic detail beyond that role is not taken from this source.

## Contributions

- Co-development of natural radiocarbon assay (attributed, Libby 1952).

## Intellectual Genealogy

Collaborator under Libby at the University of Chicago C-14 program (as described in Libby 1952).
