# Linda M. Nicholas

## Overview

Adjunct Curator in the Negaunee Integrative Research Center at the Field Museum of Natural History; long-term research partner of [[feinman-gary-m|Gary Feinman]] in Oaxaca regional survey and household excavation, and co-author of *[[blanton-ancient-oaxaca-2022|Ancient Oaxaca]]* (1999; 2nd ed. 2022).

## Contributions

- Co-author of the Valley of Oaxaca / Ejutla settlement-pattern analyses, particularly the quantitative treatment of survey data and agricultural land-use modeling.
- With Feinman, co-directed household excavations at Ejutla and El Palmillo bearing on domestic craft production and exchange in the [[zapotec-civilization|Zapotec]] region.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Career-long collaboration with Feinman, Blanton, and Kowalewski within the Oaxaca settlement-survey tradition descending from [[blanton-richard|Blanton]]'s Monte Albán survey.
