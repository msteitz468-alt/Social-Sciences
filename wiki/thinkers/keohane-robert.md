# Robert O. Keohane

## Overview

Robert O. Keohane is an American political scientist, a founding figure of neoliberal institutionalism in international relations, and co-author of *[[king-keohane-verba-designing-social-inquiry-1994|Designing Social Inquiry]]* (1994).

## Contributions

Within this wiki Keohane appears chiefly as co-architect of the KKV research-design framework — the argument that qualitative case study and comparative work should be disciplined by the same logic of [[causal-inference]] as statistical research.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Co-authored *Designing Social Inquiry* with [[king-gary]] and [[verba-sidney]].
