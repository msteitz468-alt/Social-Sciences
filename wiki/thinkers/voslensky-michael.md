# Michael Voslensky

## Overview

Sovietologist / émigré analyst associated with the English-language popularisation of **[[nomenklatura]]** as anatomy of the Soviet ruling class (*Nomenklatura*, 1984 Eng., as cited in Mawdsley & White 2000).

## Contributions

Critical-analytic use of nomenklatura as ruling-stratum label with privilege and hereditary tendencies — one of the competing designations Mawdsley and White record before choosing Central Committee membership as their operational elite.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Late-Soviet / émigré critique tradition; interlocutor for [[new-class]] and bureaucracy theses.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Can overstate class coherence; Mawdsley & White prefer measurable CC-tier elite plus job-slot structure.
