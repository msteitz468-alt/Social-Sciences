## Overview

Rex Koontz is an art historian at the University of Houston, specializing in the art and architecture of ancient Mexico, particularly El Tajín and Classic Veracruz. He is co-author, with Michael D. Coe, of *Mexico: From the Olmecs to the Aztecs* (2013), the primary source for this page (see [[sources/coe-koontz-mexico-2013]]).
