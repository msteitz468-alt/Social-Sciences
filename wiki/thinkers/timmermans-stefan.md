# Stefan Timmermans

## Overview

Sociologist of medicine/science and qualitative method; professor of sociology at UCLA (as of 2014 book bio). Birth year 1968 per *Abductive Analysis* CIP data. Coauthor with [[tavory-iddo|Iddo Tavory]] of [[abductive-analysis]].

## Contributions

- Co-developed **[[abductive-analysis]]** (*[[tavory-timmermans-abductive-analysis-2014|Abductive Analysis]]*, 2014) with [[tavory-iddo|Tavory]].
- Empirical illustrations in that book draw heavily on **Timmermans & Buchbinder** research on California expanded newborn screening (post-2006): heel-prick programs, out-of-range metabolites, “frameless” results, parental/clinic consequences-in-action, historical path from 1960s PKU screening — used as variation and causality cases (primary monographs not yet ingested).
- Other named works still outside the collection: *Postmortem*; *Saving Babies?*

## Intellectual Genealogy

Pragmatist/Peircean frame shared with Tavory; medical sociology empirical base. Joint book process described as co-rewriting. Training network not fully documented from 2014 text alone.
