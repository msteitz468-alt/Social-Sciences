# Andrew M. T. Moore

Archaeologist known for the 1972 salvage excavation of [[abu-hureyra|Abu Hureyra]] on the Euphrates (Tabqa Dam), a key sequence for late Epipalaeolithic–Neolithic plant use and [[sedentism]]. Stub from [[mithen-after-the-ice-2003|Mithen 2003]].

## Overview

Moore’s Abu Hureyra project, with specialist archaeobotany (notably [[hillman-gordon|Gordon Hillman]]), underwrites Mithen’s narrative of hunter-gatherer village life on the Euphrates and the Younger Dryas transition (attributed, Mithen 2003).

## Contributions

- Principal excavator of Abu Hureyra as a type sequence for Euphrates late Pleistocene–early Holocene settlement.
