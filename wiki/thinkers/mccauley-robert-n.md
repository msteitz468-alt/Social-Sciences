# Robert N. McCauley

## Overview

Philosopher of science and CSR co-founder with [[lawson-e-thomas]]. White (2021) places *Rethinking Religion* (1990) at the program's start and cites McCauley on the **naturalness of religion** versus the **unnaturalness of science**, and on CSR as complementing (not replacing) interpretive accounts.

## Contributions

- **Ritual form / cognitive constraints on ritual** with Lawson.
- Metatheory: explanation vs interpretation; reductionism without elimination (Slingerland–McCauley strand in White).
- Engagement with [[whitehouse-harvey|Whitehouse]] modes and ritual cognition (*Bringing Ritual to Mind*, 2002).

## Intellectual Genealogy

Collaborator: [[lawson-e-thomas]]. Network: [[whitehouse-harvey]], [[barrett-justin-l]], [[boyer-pascal]], Cohen et al. 2008 criticisms paper.

## Critiques and Reappraisals

Ritual form theory's formalism criticized as under-ethnographic; McCauley/Lawson program answers by specifying cognitive competence underlying ritual judgments, not full ritual ethnography.
