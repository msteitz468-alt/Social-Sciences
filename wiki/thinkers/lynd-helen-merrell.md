# Helen Merrell Lynd

## Overview

American sociologist and social psychologist, co-author with [[lynd-robert-s|Robert S. Lynd]] of *[[lynd-middletown-1929|Middletown]]* (1929) and *Middletown in Transition* (1937). Equal co-investigator of the Middletown field study (preface signed R.S.L. / H.M.L., New York, June 1928). Later independent work includes *On Shame and the Search for Identity* (1958) — not yet ingested; this page is seeded from the 1929 *Middletown* volume.

## Contributions

- Co-author of *Middletown*: full-community [[community-study|community study]] of [[middletown]] (fieldwork 1924–25), with the six-area activity scheme and [[business-class-and-working-class|business/working-class]] cleavage.
- Co-author of the 1937 restudy *Middletown in Transition* (not yet ingested).
- Later identity/shame social psychology (not yet ingested).

## Intellectual Genealogy

- **Middletown collaboration:** joint authorship with Robert S. Lynd; Institute of Social and Religious Research sponsorship; field staff including Faith Moors Williams (cost of living and working-class income data).
- **Students / trained:** not documented from this source alone (`[[unknown]]`).
