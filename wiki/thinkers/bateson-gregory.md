# Gregory Bateson

British anthropologist and cybernetic thinker (1904–1980). Networked in this wiki via [[mead-margaret|Margaret Mead]]'s Sepik work; via [[ingold-tim|Tim Ingold]]'s *[[ingold-perception-of-the-environment-2000|The Perception of the Environment]]* (2000); and via [[rappaport-roy-a|Rappaport]]'s *[[rappaport-ecology-meaning-religion-1979|Ecology, Meaning, and Religion]]* (1979; dedication) and *[[rappaport-ritual-religion-making-humanity-1999|Ritual and Religion in the Making of Humanity]]* (1999). Primary Bateson monographs not yet ingested.

## Overview

Bateson moves from Iatmul ethnography (*Naven*) through culture-and-personality collaborations into an "ecology of mind" that refuses the skin as the boundary of mind. Ingold records that reading Gibson recalled Bateson's insistence that "the mind… is not limited by the skin," and treats Bateson's relational ecology as a foil to Lévi-Strauss's mind-as-brain decoding model (General introduction; Ch. 1). Rappaport draws heavily on Bateson for cybernetic truth-value of propositions, adaptive response sequences, linear purposefulness vs circular systems, **deutero-learning** (→ [[deutero-truth]]), grace, play/map-territory, and the architecture of the [[cybernetics-of-the-holy]] ([[adaptive-structure]]; [[sanctity]]).

## Contributions

- **Ecology of mind** — mind in relations, not sealed in the skull (*Steps*; *Mind and Nature*).
- **Sepik ethnography** — orientation for Mead's Tchambuli ([[chambri]]) work (*[[mead-sex-and-temperament-1935|Sex and Temperament]]*).
- **Resource for [[dwelling-perspective]]** — Ingold aligns Bateson with Gibson and Merleau-Ponty on total sensory engagement (notes and Ch. 1), while noting Bateson may not have read Gibson/Merleau-Ponty.
- **[[schismogenesis]]** — progressive complementary/symmetrical differentiation through interaction (*Naven* era). Major comparative revival in [[graeber-david|Graeber]] & [[wengrow-david|Wengrow]], *[[graeber-wengrow-dawn-of-everything-2021|The Dawn of Everything]]* (2021): societies in contact join a common system of differences (California vs NW Coast; Athens–Sparta via Sahlins; Indigenous–European contact). Primary Bateson monographs still not ingested — G&W supply the present wiki depth.
- **Resource for Wagner's dialectic** — [[wagner-roy|Wagner]] (*[[wagner-invention-of-culture-1975|The Invention of Culture]]*) credits *Naven*'s "Epilogue 1936" schismogenesis as a declared debt; quotes *Naven* (2nd ed. 1958, p. 273) on Iatmul moiety "duality" as his exemplary case of a society that "mediates the conventional through the dialectic," and *Steps to an Ecology of Mind* (p. 235) on schizophrenic communication for his account of the creative double bind.
- **Cybernetic interlocutor for ecological anthropology** — as used by Rappaport (EMR 1979; RRMH 1999): validity as function of acceptance; deutero-learning → [[deutero-truth]]; not all cybernetic process is adaptive; conscious purpose vs circular systems; logical typing errors in hierarchical usurpation; grace and larger Mind motifs in Holy/adaptation chapters.
