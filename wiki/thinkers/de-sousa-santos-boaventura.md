# Boaventura de Sousa Santos

Boaventura de Sousa Santos (b. 1940) is a Portuguese sociologist of law and knowledge, associated with epistemologies of the South and critiques of Northern social science. Seeded from his long chapter "A Critique of Lazy Reason: Against the Waste of Experience" in *[[wallerstein-modern-world-system-longue-duree-2004|The Modern World-System in the Longue Durée]]* (2004).

## Overview

Santos critiques "lazy reason" that wastes the experience of the Global South and non-hegemonic knowledges; proposes expanded reason and ecology of knowledges. Claims attributed; full monographs not ingested.

## Contributions

- Critique of lazy reason / waste of experience (2004).
- Dialogue with coloniality of power (Quijano) and world-systems knowledge critique.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Critical sociology; World Social Forum milieu; dialogue with Wallersteinian [[structures-of-knowledge]].
