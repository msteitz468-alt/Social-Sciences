# Iddo Tavory

## Overview

Sociologist; at the 2014 publication of *Abductive Analysis* listed as assistant professor of sociology at New York University. Coauthor with [[timmermans-stefan|Stefan Timmermans]] of the pragmatist method program [[abductive-analysis]].

## Contributions

- Co-developed **[[abductive-analysis]]** (*[[tavory-timmermans-abductive-analysis-2014|Abductive Analysis]]*, 2014) with [[timmermans-stefan|Timmermans]]: Peircean [[abduction]] and semiotics as qualitative theory-construction program; theoretical pluralism; defamiliarization/revisiting; variation; [[consequences-in-action]]; [[community-of-inquiry]].
- Illustrative fieldwork in that book: multi-year ethnography of a Jewish Orthodox neighborhood in Los Angeles’s Melrose area (including living there); street identification / category-activation puzzles used to show multi-theory recasing.

## Intellectual Genealogy

Philosophical lineage to C. S. Peirce and [[pragmatism]]; methodological foils [[grounded-theory]] and [[extended-case-method]]. Acknowledgments in the 2014 book name extended interlocutors (e.g. Adele Clarke, Richard Bernstein, and others) — training genealogy not fully reconstructable from that text alone. Joint rewriting with Timmermans described as washing out personal idiosyncrasies.
