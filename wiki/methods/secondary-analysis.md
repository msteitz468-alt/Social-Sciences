# Secondary Analysis

Reanalysis of existing survey archives, administrative series, and published data for new questions. Exemplary large-scale use: [[putnam-bowling-alone-study|*Bowling Alone*]] (Roper, GSS, DDB Needham, membership series, time-use).

## What It Does

Extracts trend and association claims from data collected for other purposes.

## Procedure

Archive selection → item harmonization → trend estimation → triangulation across independent sources (Putnam's "global warming" metaphor).

## Assumptions and Limitations

Continuity of meaning of items; representativeness; ecological inference risks when moving from individual items to "community" capital.

## History

Grew with ICPSR and national omnibus surveys; central to late-20th-c. civic-engagement research.
